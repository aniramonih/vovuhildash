Inside Farrah Abraham's New Luxury Home

Farrah Abraham loves to live a lavish lifestyle. Although some may question how she can afford to do so, Farrah doesn’t cut corners when it comes to having expensive things.

She goes on luxury vacations, hangs out on yachts, and wears everything designer. And now, she’s showing off her new apartment!

The $4,000 a month Los Angeles pad has everything one may (or may not) need.

Credit: MTV

Since August 2019, Farrah and Sophia have been living in a 1 bed, 2 bath apartment for $4,013 a month, The Sun reports.

The 945-square foot apartment has a modern kitchen equipped with stainless steel appliances, as well as floor to ceiling windows in the living room. Additionally, there is a balcony for outdoor space.

The complex features a pool, which has been seen on Farrah’s social media.

The apartment building also has a spa and gym which are open to all tenants.

Farrah and Sophia spend plenty of time in this apartment since it was recently revealed that Farrah homeschools her daughter. The two also filmed a copious amount of TikTok videos in the apartment and around the building.

In a video shared to reddit, fans were not excited to see what Farrah Abraham was up to.

She was working out in a sports bra and bike shorts while wearing a visor and medical mask. But her “workout” was definitely unconventional…she was bouncing on an exercise ball while pulling on a weight machine.

“Farrah is going to start teaching Jazzercise with her degree from Havard. Her coordination is tops!” the original poster wrote on the video.

“What is this? Seriously, WTF is she doing?!” one fan wrote.

“This can’t be real,” added another. “There is a lot to unpack here god d*mn.”

“What in the hell did I just watch?” added a third.

Others said “This makes me so uncomfy” and “This is so absolutely chaotic.”
