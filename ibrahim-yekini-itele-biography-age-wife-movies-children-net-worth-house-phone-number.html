Biography

Ibrahim Yekini Bakare (born January 21, 1980), also known by his stage name Itele D Icon, is a highly acclaimed Yoruba actor and TV personality in Nigeria. 

His exceptional talent and skills as an actor, screenwriter, movie producer, and director have significantly impacted the entertainment industry. Ibrahim Yekini Itele gained immense recognition for his excellent performances in movies such as Kesari, Itele, and Romeo. 

Throughout his career, Ibrahim Yekini Itele has consistently proven himself to be a versatile and dedicated artist, captivating audiences with his remarkable portrayals on the silver screen.

Early Life & Education

Ibrahim Yekini Itele was born in Bariga, Lagos, Nigeria, on January 21, 1980. He hails from a Nigerian village, Adimula, Osun State. He was raised by his loving parents throughout his childhood and had the opportunity to enjoy the joys and challenges of growing up with his siblings. 

While Ibrahim Yekini Itele has succeeded in various fields, he has kept personal details private, refusing to share anything about his parents or siblings with the public.

Ibrahim Yekini Itele completed his education at Ahmadiyah Secondary School, obtaining his Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) and First Leaving School Certificate. Afterward, he decided to further his studies by enrolling at Moshood Abiola Polytechnic in Abeokuta (MAPOLY).

Career

In 2005, Ibrahim Yekini Itele embarked on a journey into Nigeria’s vibrant film realm, Nollywood. He took his first steps before the camera in the movie “Oro Abere,” directed by the esteemed Tunde Kelani.

Ibrahim Yekini Itele, a skilled filmmaker, has left his mark as a scriptwriter, director, and producer. Notable works like Anini, Akoba, Kesari, and Lucifer showcase his prowess. In 2019, he achieved a milestone with Return Of Kesari, where he held multiple roles. His recent Father Abraham project adds to his impressive body of work.

Beyond acting, Ibrahim Yekini Itele co-directed a film with Tope Adebayo, resulting in the eagerly awaited Ololufe, set to release this year. With his ongoing success, Ibrahim Yekini Itele continues to explore new creative avenues.

Personal Life

Ibrahim Yekini Itele, aged 44, tied the knot with his wife, Olayiwola Aminat Omowunmi, in June 2016, and they became parents to a child together.

After welcoming a daughter with Olayiwola Aminat Omowunmi in 2019, rumors circulated that Ibrahim Yekini Itele D Icon was considering a second marriage. Allegedly, he was set to marry a video vixen shortly after the birth of his child. However, this turned out to be a baseless rumor.

During Olayiwola Aminat Omowunmi‘s birthday celebrations, Ibrahim Yekini Itele took to his Instagram to express his admiration for her, acknowledging her role as a wife, mother, and homemaker. This suggests her support for his successful career in the film industry.

Ibrahim Yekini Itele and his wife welcomed their first child, Ameerat Bakare, in 2018, followed by a baby girl in April 2022. The details of the baby girl’s name remain private. Ibrahim was previously married to Dupe Yekini, but their marriage ended in divorce.

Before his relationship with Olayiwola Aminat Omowunmi, Ibrahim was married to Dupe Yekini, and they reportedly had four children together. Following the end of their marriage, Dupe Yekini took their children with her under the understanding that Ibrahim would provide for them. However, online reports suggested Ibrahim had not met his responsibilities, prompting accusations of neglect.

Initially denying these claims, Ibrahim Yekini Itele revealed undisclosed health challenges affecting his finances. He attributed his initial response to frustration and expressed regret for it. He promised to mend the situation and fulfill his responsibilities towards his family moving forward. Time will reveal whether he follows through with his commitment.
