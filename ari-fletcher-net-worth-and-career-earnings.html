Ari Fletcher Net Worth and Career Earnings

Ari Fletcher net worth is $3 million. She makes her money majorly from her hair company, KYCHE Extension. She also earns considerably from her modeling and social media influencing career.

Ari Fletcher is a 28-year-old American businesswoman, social media influencer, YouTuber, and model. She owns and runs her own hair company known as KYCHE Extension. Not only that, but Ari has also earned a spot among the most successful social media influencers and Instagram models. Take a look at how she built this wealth and fame.

What is Ari Fletcher’s Net Worth Today?

Many online sources have reported different figures regarding Ari Fletcher’s net worth. While some peg it at $10 million, others report it to be $2 million, while another set report $3 million. In any case, although she herself has not ever declared her net worth publicly, the fact remains that Ari Fletcher’s net worth certainly runs in millions of dollars.

Ari Fletcher is the definition of ‘multitalented’. She has carved a niche for herself at such a young age and is indeed reaping the fruit of her hard work. Her dedication to her passion for business and fashion has seen her work and dine with notable personalities and brands across the States, and from what is perceivable, this is the beginning of her success.

How Did Ari Fletcher Become a Millionaire?

Ari Fletcher became a billionaire through her various entrepreneurial ventures. She landed her first paid job as a waitress at Adrianna’s Nightclub in Chicago, Illinois after her high school education. She worked there for a few months before she got enrolled at Northern Illinois University. Then, after she dropped out of the university, she kicked off her social media career.

Arin Fletcher Earns Decently From Social Media Influencing and Modeling

Ari Fletcher first gained recognition on various social media platforms where all her accounts are monetized. She created her Instagram account in 2014 and started modeling on the page. Meanwhile, while she was working at Adrianna’s Nightclub, she was reportedly noticed by some modeling scouts who would later help her launch her modeling career.

She simply was posting pictures of herself on her page modeling, showcasing her curvy physique, and dapper fashion sense. Ari has all the while been a self-confident woman and that earned her the attention of many brands, both the nominal and highly rated ones.

In no time, the number of her followers drastically rose and over the years, she has worked with such brands as Fashion Nova and Savage X Fenty. Also, thanks to her social media fame, she has made appearances in some print publications. In 2021, she graced the cover of Vogue magazine.

YouTube is Another Source of Ari Fletcher’s Income

Fletcher also earns from YouTube. She subsequently created a YouTube channel where she started posting vlogs of her day-to-day life. Significantly, she makes money from sponsored/paid Instagram and YouTube ads and promotions. Each sponsored post she makes reportedly earns her a one-time payment.

Ari makes a significant amount of money by advertising popular products on her YouTube channel which has over 500k subscribers and her Instagram account where she has 5.9 million followers. While the exact amount she makes from these platforms is not clear, the businesswoman while speaking with Alfro Tech revealed that she has made more than one million dollars on her Instagram account.

She is also active on Facebook and Twitter with about 3 million and 2 million followers respectively. In addition, Ari Fletcher has assisted other individuals in turning a profit from their platforms. She assisted her friend Mariie, the CEO of Moon Cosmetics, to monetize her own platform. Similarly, she assisted her makeup artist, Olivia Song, in advancing her beauty brand, Supernatural Cosmetics.

KYCHE Extension

Having established herself as a social media personality, Ari Fletcher decided to venture into the business world, precisely in the beauty and hair weave industry. Alongside her cousin, Britney, she founded a hair extension company called KYCHE Extensions in 2016. The company is named after Ari’s brother named Kyle Jamison who passed away in 2013 while she was attending Chicago Vocational High School.

Ari consistently advertises her company’s products on all her social media platforms. According to reports, the KYCHE Extensions company has since grown into a multimillion company with a net worth of over $10 million. Her wide online recognition has helped her expand her hair business to an international level.

REMEDY BY ARI

Another business venture Ari Fletcher is known for is her top-selling beauty band, REMEDY BY ARI. Fletcher is a self-taught makeup enthusiast who learned her way around YouTube, and so decided to also make a business off it. She is said to have worked on the beauty brand for two years before launching it in August 2022.

Just three hours after the online launch, customers purchased all the products, leaving others waiting till the next restock. According to Fletcher, she was completely blown away by such huge support and patronage for her brand. Arin Fletcher is a self-made millionaire. She makes six-figure-plus deals with brands and media companies.

Needless to say that her fortune is set to boost in the nearest future. She has over the years gathered an in-depth experience from a series of business meetings with top luminaries. She is a social media influencer who has carefully learned her ropes and mastered the business of influencer marketing in and out, and ultimately earns big through it.

Fletcher prides herself on being strong-minded and disciplined. She constantly has the admiration of her fans due to her bold and beautiful look. Meanwhile, she recently revealed that she is currently learning about customer service, e-commerce, and logistics to expand her expertise.
