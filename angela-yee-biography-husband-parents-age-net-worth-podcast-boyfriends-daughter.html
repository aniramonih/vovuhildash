Angela Yee Biography: Husband, Parents, Age, Net Worth, Podcast, Boyfriends, Daughter

Biography

Angela Yee (born January 3, 1976) is an American radio personality currently hosting the iHeartRadio syndicated radio show Way Up. She began her radio odyssey as an intern at Wu-Tang Management, collaborating with stars like Method Man and Ghostface Killah.

Her trajectory led her to Sirius XM Radio, where she co-hosted The Cipha Sounds & Rosenberg Show with Cipha Sounds and Peter Rosenberg. 

Angela Yee‘s radio prowess earned her accolades, including being recognized as one of Essence Magazine’s Top Women in Hip Hop.

Early Life & Education

Angela Yee, born on January 3, 1976, in Brooklyn, New York City, hails from a diverse heritage, with a Chinese father, Mr. Yee, and an Afro-Montserratian mother, Celestina Farage-Yee. Her Brooklyn upbringing saw her attending the prestigious Poly Prep Country Day School before pursuing an English degree at Wesleyan University.

During her Wesleyan years, Angela Yee‘s interests extended beyond academics. She ventured into campus radio hosting, igniting her passion for media. 

Post-graduation, Angela Yee‘s journey led her through various roles in the music industry, eventually finding her calling in radio.

Career

Angela Yee‘s journey in the media industry is a remarkable tale of resilience and versatility. It all began when she interned at Wu-Tang Management, where she gained invaluable experience working with renowned artists like Method Man and Ghostface Killah. Her early involvement in the music scene, including contributing to GZA‘s album Beneath the Surface, laid the foundation for her future success.

Angela Yee‘s career path led her through various radio stations, from Hot 97 to Shade 45, before landing at Power 105.1 in 2010. Her magnetic presence and insightful interviews on shows like The Cipha Sounds and Rosenberg Show and later The Breakfast Club catapulted her into media stardom.

Beyond radio, Angela Yee founded the engaging lifestyle brand Angela Yee’s Lip Service. She hosts a podcast by the same name, fearlessly tackling topics of sex and relationships with diverse guests.

Angela Yee ventured into television with appearances on VH1’s The Gossip Game and BET’s 106 & Park, further solidifying her status as a multifaceted media figure.

Her remarkable journey culminated in recognition from Forbes’ 30 Under 30 in the media category. Angela Yee‘s impact on hip-hop media is undeniable, as she supports emerging artists and uses her platform to address critical social issues. She’s a vocal advocate for social justice and women’s empowerment, leveraging her voice for positive change.

Angela Yee also authored The Morning Hustle: 10 Habits for the 20-Year Hustler, a valuable resource for navigating the entertainment industry.

Personal Life

Angela Yee and her friend, Sara Golan of Nest Seekers International, purchased a Catskills home in upstate New York for $1.9 million. This mid-century-modern mansion sits on 9.5 acres in Roxbury and offers seven bedrooms and four bathrooms.

In her personal life, Angela Yee has been in relationships with Gucci Mane (2016), Plies (2015), and Joyner Lucas (2015). Currently, she does not have any children and has never been married.
