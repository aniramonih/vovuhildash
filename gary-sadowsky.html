Gary Sadowsky Biography

Gary Sadowsky is an American Meteorologist, who was born and brought up in Twin Cities, Minnesota, United States of America. Currently, he works at WCAX in Burlington, Vermont as a meteorologist, joining as a weekend weathercaster, then moved on 11:00 PM weekdays, and later the Channel 3 Early Morning News. Previously he was a bicycle shop manager, before pursuing his career in meteorology. He is a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist, American Meteorological Society.
