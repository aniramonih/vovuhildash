FAQs about Ras Kwame

Who is Ras Kwame?

He is a British musician, record producer, radio DJ, and presenter known for his Ghanaian heritage. He has been a significant figure in the UK’s music scene, particularly in genres like reggae and UK Garage.

What is he known for?

He is renowned for his work as a DJ, music producer, and radio presenter. He has contributed significantly to the reggae and UK Garage scenes and is a staple on Capital XTRA with his show, the Reggae Recipe.

How did he start his career?

He began his career as a club DJ in the early 1990s, playing hip hop, R&B, and reggae, and later moved into music production and radio presenting.

What shows does he host?

Where can I find Ras Kwame online?

He is active on social media platforms like Instagram (@raskwame) and Twitter (@RASKWAME), and he also has content on YouTube and SoundCloud.

He studied economics at the City of London Polytechnic after moving back to London from Ghana where he spent part of his childhood.

He was born in Hammersmith, London, and he is of Ghanaian descent.

He founded Baby Shack Recordings and has been involved with other music production projects through his career.

What other activities is he known for?

He is a music industry consultant and voice actor, and he regularly participates as a judge in music competitions like Open Mic UK and Live and Unsigned.
