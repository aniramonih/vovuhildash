Christopher Titus Wife, Real Dad, Mom, Kids, Girlfriend

Growing up for Christopher Titus wasn’t like every other thrilling childhood story. It was difficult for Christopher, he had to tolerate the unpleasant inconvenience in his family, his father being an alcoholic and his mother suffering from schizophrenia. She was later acquitted of the murder of her second husband and also committed suicide. So far, he has managed to excel going through a difficult childhood experience and today he has become a successful actor and talented comedian.

Christopher Titus Biography

American actor and comedian ChristopherTodd Titus was born one of three children to Ken Titus and Juanita Titus on October 1, 1964 in Newark, California. He is well known for being the star of the show Titus on the Fox network. His show was previously described by Times magazine as a brutal, hilarious and daring show.

Growing up, Christopher lived with his parents and two other siblings, Dave Titus and Shannon Titus, in Castro Valley, California. His parents divorced when he was still a toddler, his father was granted custody of him, as well as that of his siblings, from his mother, who was then suffering from psychosis.

Christopher Titus began his career as a comedian sharing jokes about his past and personal experiences, focusing on domestic violence, custody battle, family suicides, heart attacks and more. He made his first television appearance on the show Norman Rockwell is Bleeding. He had huge success with many TV shows like his first show Norman Rockwell bleeds and few other shows.

His show Neverlution was his fourth comedy show, he talked about politics and restoring the country’s lost glories. On one of his other stand-up shows, Love is Evol, he discussed his divorce from ex-wife Erin, his relationships, and when he started dating again.

The Voice in My Head was the show that came after Neverlution. It was first named S carred for life before it was later changed to Epic Fail and eventually The Voice in My Head. The show focused on Christopher’s experience, both successful ones and unsuccessful ones.

wife, girlfriend

Christopher Titus married his first wife Erin Carden on April 17, 1991. The couple first dated three years after they first met in 1988. After spending fifteen years together, they called it quits on June 6, 2006 .

After his divorce from Erin, Christopher took some time to date but find love again with Rachel Bradley. After dating for two years, the couple married on August 11, 2013. Rachel Bradley was born on October 3, 1977.

Christopher’s real father and mother

Christopher Titus is the son of Kenneth Williams Titus. Kenneth Williams Titus was born on October 6, 1942. Ken was an alcoholic, but that didn’t stop him from winning custody of these three children from his mentally deranged wife during their divorce. Ken died on January 6, 2001 at the age of 58.

Kenneth Titus’ wife and Christopher’s mother, Juanita Clarice Holmes, as she would later be called following her remarriage, was born on April 21, 1930, in Missouri, United States. She was a pianist and had three children with Ken Titus before their divorce. Christopher and his siblings were largely raised by their father who, like Juanita, had several relationships, but at age 12 Christopher left his father to live with his mother in his garage. Shortly after, he returned with his father. Growing up, Christopher Titus almost followed in his father’s footsteps. He struggled with alcohol and drugs until one day while drunk he fell into a bonfire at a beach party and the next day morning, his friends found him and took him to the hospital.

Christopher’s mother, Juanita, was previously charged with killing her second husband, but the jury found her innocent and she was acquitted of the murder case. Christopher lost his mother on November 18, 1994. She committed suicide at the age of 64.

Christopher’s Kids

The comedian and actor has two children, Kennie Marie Titus, born August 25, 2001, and Jett Evan Titus, born May 8, 2004. He had both children with his first wife, Erin Carden.
