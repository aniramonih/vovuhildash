American Actress Katee Sackhoff relationship status after breakup with Karl Urban

From battling aliens in her movies to battling cancer in real life, Katee Sackhoff is winning everywhere. The beautiful actress was engaged in 2012 with film producer Scott Niemeyer. 

What happened after that? Are the couple happily married till now? Well, if your head is going through these questions, then you are in the right place. Let's know more about her boyfriend and her relationship.

Is Katee Sackhoff's a Married Woman?

Battlestar Galactica actress Sackhoff is not married. In 2012, she was engaged to TV producer Scott Niemeyer but never married. Nearly two years after that in 2014, the actress started a new chapter in her life with a brand new relationship.

She started her romantic affair with her co-actor from Star Trek, Karl Urban. No, guys, he is not related to famous singer Keith Urban by any means. The couple is very comfortable with each other and is also familiar with each other's family.

Karl Urban's second attempt to find love

After a failed marriage, Karl Urban is all set to find love again. Prior to Katee, he tied the knot with Natalie Wihongi in September 2004. The couple seemed very happy in their marital relationship but a decade later, they announced their separation.

The reason for their divorce is not shared. In June 2014, the pair announced their split by providing a joint statement,

"It is with great regret that we, Karl Urban and Natalie Wihongi, have made the mutual decision to separate. We are committed to maintaining our family relationship, working together to ensure that our children are affected as little as possible. We request that people respect our family's privacy at this time and will be making no further comment".

Karl is the father of two children with Natalie, Hunter Urban, and Indiana Urban. On the other hand, Katee also was in a seven-year relationship with producer Scott Niemeyer. The pair was so in love that they announced their engagement in 2012. But it didn't last as the pair called off their engagement and ended their relationship.

Katee Sackhoff in the Romantic picture...

After the divorce with Natalie, Urban found love again in the same year. In 2014, Karl and Katee announced their love for each other and appeared in many events together. The couple is very happily enjoying each other's company, however, they have not shared any details about how they meet each other.

Both work in the showbiz industry and has worked together in movies previously. We can assume the pair might've met each other in one of these film set and blossomed their affair. After a year of publicizing their romance in public, Urban decided to take Sackhoff his home to introduce her to his family.

Urban's children are also very familiar with her now and it looks like all is set for the couple to take the next step. Within these years, the pair's love for each other has grown which can be seen on their Instagram pics. Nearly after four years of togetherness, in 2018, as per many online sources, the couple announced their separation.

Many of their fans are surely disappointed as they wanted to see the pair getting engaged and eventually getting married. But that's not going to happen now, moreover, they have also not shared any details about their separation.

Her Current Relationship Status

As of now, her relationship status is single. Sackhoff is enjoying her life and especially after fighting thyroid cancer in 2004, she is starting to appreciate her life more. Every day, she takes her medication and tries to make healthy lifestyle choices.

After the tumor surgery in 2004, she said,

"I think the thing that changed my life so much was just the idea that life is very finite, and cancer had touched me at what I thought was a very young age".

She further added,

"Since then, I just take my thyroid medication on a daily basis and live a full, happy, healthy life that is relatively smooth at this point".
