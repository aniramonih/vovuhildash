Who is Mary Padian From Storage Wars and What Happened To Her?

Mary Padian Was Born in 1980

Mary Padian is 43 years old and was born on August 24, 1980, in Dallas, Texas. Mary developed a passion for antiques and collectibles from a young age. She attended the University of Texas at Austin, where she graduated with a degree in photojournalism.

Mary Padian’s upbringing played a significant role in shaping her love for antiques. Growing up in a creative household, her father, John Padian, was an artist, and her mother, Teresa Padian, was a journalist. These influences nurtured her passion for storytelling and finding beauty in the old and forgotten.

After completing her studies, Mary joined her family’s business, Padian Works, where she worked alongside her father, John Padian, who is also an artist. During this time, she gained valuable experience in refurbishing furniture and repurposing old items, which further fueled her interest in antiques.

She Is One of the Stars of The A&E Show, Storage Wars

Mary’s career in the world of antiques began when she interned at a local store called Gypsy Trader. This experience further fueled her love for unique and vintage items. She honed her skills by attending numerous auctions and estate sales, learning to spot hidden treasures and negotiate the best deals. Mary’s eye for valuable items and her competitive spirit quickly made her stand out in the world of antiques.

In 2010, Mary Padian got her big break when she joined the cast of the reality TV show Storage Wars Texas. The show follows a group of buyers who bid on abandoned storage lockers with the hope of finding valuable items inside. Mary quickly became a fan favorite due to her unique sense of style and her ability to uncover hidden gems.

Throughout her career, Mary Padian has received recognition for her contributions to the business of antique collections and reality television. Her expertise in finding rare and valuable items has earned her the well-deserved nickname “The Queen of Bargains.”

Mary’s keen eye for antiques and collectibles has led her to discover remarkable finds on Storage Wars Texas, including vintage furniture, unique art pieces, and valuable memorabilia. Her ability to turn these discoveries into profitable ventures has solidified her reputation as one of the most successful cast members on the show.

What Happened To Mary Padian from Storage Wars?

Despite her success, Mary Padian’s journey has not been without challenges. In recent years, she had to take a break from Storage Wars. Although the specifics of these challenges have not been publicly disclosed, it is evident that she prioritized her well-being and took time away to focus on herself.

Firstly, Mary was off-air for a while after A&E canceled Storage Wars in 2014. Later, the network gave in to viewer requests to air Mary again and did so through Season 13. In 2019, Storage Wars was suspended indefinitely. During her absence from the show, Mary continued to engage with her fans through social media, sharing updates on her life and projects. Her followers expressed their support and eagerly awaited her return to the screen.

On March 8, 2022, when the show’s 14th season premiered, fans were expecting to see Mary Padian however, she was nowhere to be found. However, it should be noted that during Season 13 of the show, Mary changed from one of the leading buyers to a guest star, which means she will appear on the show much less frequently.

Mary Padian Has Her Own Antique Store in Dallas 

While Storage Wars Texas brought Mary Padian into the spotlight, she has also pursued various business ventures outside of the show. In addition to her work on Storage Wars, Mary owns a store, Mary’s Finds, located in Dallas, Texas. The store offers a curated selection of unique and vintage items that Mary discovers at auctions and estate sales.

Mary is an avid photographer and frequently incorporates her love for photography into her work. She has showcased her photography skills by creating custom art pieces using her photographs. These pieces often feature vintage themes and serve as a testament to her artistic talent.

Furthermore, Mary has collaborated with fellow Storage Wars cast member Moe Prigoff to launch a successful online shop called Mary’s Closet Vintage. The shop features an array of vintage clothing and accessories she sourced herself.

In addition to her business endeavors, Mary has made guest appearances on other television shows, further expanding her presence in the entertainment industry. She has been a guest on talk shows and has shared her expertise in antiques and collectibles on various platforms.
