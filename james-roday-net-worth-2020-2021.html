Early Life

James Roday is the son of Irene Rodriguez and Jaime “Jim” Rodriguez, who gave birth to him in the year 1976 in San Antonio, located in Texas.

His father was of Mexican origin, whereas his mother was of Irish, English, and Scottish origin. His father is known to be a retired Air Force Master Sergeant, and he used to work as the regional catering executive of Taco Cabana.

Roday went to Taft High School, located in San Antonio. Later, he went to New York University’s Experimental Theatre Wing, and there he studied theatre and got a bachelor’s degree in the field of fine arts.

It was during his age of 22 that he chose the professional name, i.e. James Roday, because there was already another “James Rodriguez” name registered within the Screen Actors Guild.
