RNC Regional Engagement Coordinator Cale Lowery dies suddenly

Fairmont, NC – The community grieves the unexpected passing of Cale Lowery, a cherished resident of Fairmont, North Carolina, and a Regional Engagement Coordinator at the Republican National Committee Headquarters. His beloved wife, Alina Rose Lowery, shared the heart-wrenching news via social media, expressing her profound grief and the devastating loss of her loving husband.

Cale Lowery, born and raised in Fairmont, North Carolina, was a devoted son to Amy Lowery and Cale Lowery Sr. He attended South Robeson High School and Purnell Swett High School, as indicated on his social media profile. Cale and Alina Rose Lowery were united in marriage on March 11th, 2023.

Professionally, Cale Lowery made significant contributions as the Regional Engagement Coordinator at the Republican National Committee Headquarters. His dedication to public service extended to a part-time role as a Safety Board Committee Member at the North Carolina Farm Bureau Mutual Insurance Company from 2021 to 2023.

Lowery’s interest in politics blossomed early, sparked by witnessing US President Barack Obama’s first inauguration at the age of six in 2009. This ignited his passion for political science, leading him to pursue studies at The University of North Carolina at Pembroke, majoring in public policy and administration.
