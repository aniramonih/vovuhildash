What is Hank Haney's Net Worth?

Hank Haney is an American professional golf instructor who has a net worth of $10 million dollars. Hank Haney is based in Dallas, Texas, where he runs four golf training centers. He graduated from the University of Tulsa with a degree in Education, and began working under a range of golf pros. After serving as a Director of Golf and a Director of Instruction at multiple large training centers, he opened his own school in 1991. Over the years, as his training centers have multiplied and grown, he has coached over 200 professional golfers.

He is known for improving the mechanics of all of his students' golf swings. Some of his famous former and current students include Tiger Woods and Mark O'Meara. He has his own reality show called, "The Haney Project", in which he coaches celebrities towards a better golf game. He is also the subject of the videogame, "Hank Haney's World Golf" (a.k.a. "World Challenge Golf 2011").
