Who is reality star, Mariah Torres dating after divorced with her first husband, Marcel?

Remarkable social media personality Mariah Torres is one of the well-known reality stars. She is widely remembered for her appearance in Animal Planet's series Pit Bulls & Parolees. Her helping hands for recusing dogs have been most appreciated by millions and millions of people from all around the world which has helped her to gain a huge amount of fan following in a matter of time. 

Twenty-nine years old, Reality star, Mariah Torres was born on 6th September 1991 in California, United States. She and her three siblings were raised by working-class parents who run Villalobos Rescue Center which is the Largest pit bull animal shelter in the US. Her mother Tia Torres is also a familiar face in the TV world.

Her sister, Tania, and twin brothers Keli'i and Kanani are also part of the particular rescue center. Furthermore, at the age of eleven, she performed in various beauty pageants.

The current relationship status of Mariah Torres - Single

The beautiful Instagram influencer, Mariah has been always one of the highlights in the media world. Her unique looks full with tattoo has helped her to get extra attention. So, many fans of her always wonder whether she is engaged in many affairs and relationships, however, the wait is over. Torres's current relationship status is marked as single and she hasn't been involved in any sort of relationship. 

According to her, she recently got single and is quite enjoying her own companionship. Furthermore, she is still busy working with her family in the various dog rescue program. Also, she loves to travel and explore places with her mates and often uploads her day-to-day lifestyle in her official Instagram account. 

Failed marriage with ex-husband, Marcel

Highlighting the relationship of Marcel and Mariah they two met one another at Villalobos rescue center. Marcel used to work as a volunteer in that particular center where the ex-couple started their beautiful relationship. They even have recused numerous dogs from a very difficult situation. On Pit Bulls & Parolees show, Marcel even rescued the puppies from the apartment with fire. 

Having the same working environment and by knowing each other deeply the couple started dating and fell in love. In 2016 in Vegas the couple accepted one another as husband and wife. However, after enjoying nuptial life for a couple of months the pair got separated and the news shocked the whole fans. 

In 2018 they officially got divorced and went their separate ways. As of now, Mariah is enjoying life as single while there is no news regarding, Marcel having any affairs and relationship.
