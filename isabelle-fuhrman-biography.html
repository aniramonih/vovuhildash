Isabelle Fuhrman Bio (Wiki)

Who is Isabelle Fuhrman?

Young and beautiful Isabelle Fuhrman is an American actress who has started her career in 2004. She is best known for her appearance in the films like Orphan, The Hunger Games, and Dear Eleanor.

Isabelle Fuhrman’s early life, childhood, and education

The daughter of Elina Fuhrman and Nick Fuhrman, Isabelle Fuhrman was born in Washington, D.C, and raised in Atlanta, Georgia. She was born on 25 February 1997 and grew up with her sister Madeline Fuhrman.

Her mother s a journalist and her father is a former political candidate and business consultant. She is of Russian Jewish ethnicity and has an American nationality.

She studied at Buckley School and graduated from  Stanford University. She also studied at RADA and briefly attended The Westminster Schools in Atlanta, Georgia. She stands 1.6 m and has dark hair.

In 2007, she made her big-screen debut in the drama Hounddog portraying the character of Grasshopper. Her career has started to blossom in the entertainment field.

She appeared in the television movie Children of the Corn in 2009 and Pleading the Guilty in 2010. Her career took on a successful path when she was landed the role of Clove in the movie The Hunger Games in 2012.
