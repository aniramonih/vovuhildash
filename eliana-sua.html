Eliana Sua

Eliana Sua is an American actress.

Eliana is a fourth cousin, once removed, of wrestler Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson. Eliana’s paternal great-great-great-great-grandparents, William Crichton and Uputaua Falemalu Lesa, were also Dwayne’s maternal great-great-great-grandparents.

Eliana’s paternal grandfather is Murphy Suluga Su’a (Monte Alexander Crichton/Sane Kipeni Su’apa’ia and Paolosolonuu Mosese Matatia “Millie” Leapai). Murphy was born in Mapusaga, Samoa. Monte was the son of Kipeni Saimasina Su’apa’ia, who was part Tongan, and of Lisi Elizabeth Luisa Crichton, whose paternal grandfather was Scottish. Paolosolonuu was the daughter of Matatia Le’apai, who was part Tongan, and of Ema Siositeni Maiava, who was born on New Britain, Papua New Guinea, to Samoan parents.

Eliana’s paternal grandmother is Anita Yavette “Nina” Martinez (the daughter of Paul P. Martinez and Carmen Jovita Lopez). Anita was born in California, of Mexican descent. Paul was the son of Manuel Martinez and Josephine R Silva. Carmen was the daughter of Isaac Lopez and Julia Robles.

Eliana’s maternal grandfather is named Carlos Antonio Sanchez. Carlos was born in Argentina.

Eliana’s maternal grandmother is Marta Y. Sal (the daughter of Julio Santos Sal and Francesca Faviana). Marta was born in Argentina. Julio was the son of Vicente Julio Sal and Rosa Filomena Palomares. Francesca was born in Sicily, Italy, the daughter of Salvatore Faviana and Concetta Eterno.
