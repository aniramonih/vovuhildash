Caoimhin Kelleher Age And Parents Ethnicity

As for Caoimhin Kelleher's age, he is 23 years old.

He was born to Ray and Jacqueline Kelleher on 23 November 1998. His father, a former player and trainer of Dohenys GAA Club, and his grandfather, Timothy, were from Dunmanway. His father passed away in 2014.

Caoimhin's dad was the reason behind him playing the goalkeeper position. He reportedly called his son's youth coach, Eddie Harrington, and asked him to put him in goal, and he did, halfway through the Under-14s season. Before that, he was a good center forward. 

As for his mom, there isn't much detail about her online. Nonetheless, reports suggest that Stephen Bermingham met her, setting off a chain of events that ultimately led to the footballer starting a game for Liverpool in the Champions League.

In addition to his parents, Kelleher has three older brothers. One of his siblings, Fiacre, is also a professional footballer who plays the position of a defender.
