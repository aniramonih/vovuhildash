Pauline Jani dies aged 34: Cause of death, obituary and funeral arrangements

Pauline Jani was a notable Zimbabwean netball player recognized for her versatility on the court, excelling in the roles of goal keeper and goal shooter. She was born on July 10, 1989. She is originally from Hippo Valley Estate in Zimbabwe and has represented her country on multiple occasions.

Her participation in the 2019 Netball World Cup was a watershed point in her career because it signified Zimbabwe’s first-ever appearance in a Netball World Cup tournament. Pauline Jani was an important element of Zimbabwe’s squad, which competed courageously and finished in eighth place.

Pauline Jani declared her retirement from sport at the age of 30 after playing an important role in Zimbabwe’s final group stage match against Uganda at the 2019 World Cup. Her contributions to the game left an unforgettable imprint on Zimbabwean netball, and her sportsmanship inspired many aspiring athletes.

Pauline Jani’s life was tragically cut short when she died on October 11, 2023, at the age of 34, in Parirenyatwa Hospital in Harare. Her legacy in netball and impact on the sport will be treasured and cherished by her teammates and fans alike.
