TikTok Star Carl Eiswerth Dies at 35 in a Tragic Car Crash!

Carl Eiswerth dies in a car crash. source: Page Six

How did TikToker Carl Eiswerth die?

At the young age of 35, TikToker Carl Eiswerth passed away in a tragic car accident.

The TikToker’s mother, Janet, confirmed the news to TMZ and explained when and how the accident occurred.

She revealed that her son and his friend were driving along a Pennsylvania intersection when another vehicle hit their car.

Furthermore, the Synder County Deputy Coroner told WKOK that the TikToker who was in the passenger seat died on the spot due to blunt trauma force.

After the crash, the state police shut down Route 11 for about 4 miles.

Carl’s mom told that his family is planning two memorial services and they will try to live stream both events so his fans can pay their respects to the late social media influencer.

Eiswerth was famous on Instagram under the username @team_carl_forever. He had more than 435,400 followers at the time of his passing.

Janet also revealed they still have no idea what they will do with his social media channels.

His TikTok contents were mainly about him living his everyday life, dancing to famous songs, and opening up about mental health. He loved going live on TikTok and did that just two days before his accident.
