The Meaning Behind The Song: Kick Doe Click by That Mexican OT

As a musician, I’ve come across countless songs that have touched me in different ways. However, there are certain tracks that resonate with me on a deeper level. One such song is “Kick Doe Click” by That Mexican OT. I remember stumbling upon this song at a friend’s house, and from the moment it started playing, I was captivated.

The lyrics of “Kick Doe Click” delve into the life experiences and mindset of the artist, That Mexican OT. The track begins with a catchy chorus, where That Mexican OT proudly declares his affiliation with the “Kick Doe Click” and his determination to never go back to a state of poverty. It sets the tone for the rest of the song, which is a raw and honest portrayal of a life filled with struggles and victories.

The verses in “Kick Doe Click” paint vivid pictures of the artist’s journey. That Mexican OT recounts riding around with his Mexican friends, all of them armed and ready for any situation. They’re on a money mission, counting digits and strictly focused on business. These lines speak to the artist’s hustle and determination to make it to the top, despite the obstacles in his way.

That Mexican OT doesn’t shy away from the harsh realities of his life. He talks about beating someone on camera and charging them for their mistakes. He describes himself as a scary individual, capable of taking out anyone who poses a threat. These lines reflect the artist’s street persona, as well as his ability to survive and thrive in a tough environment.

Through the lyrics of “Kick Doe Click,” That Mexican OT also highlights his cultural identity. He proudly mentions being Mexican and compares himself to a lawnmower, ready to clear out any snakes in his path. This line showcases the artist’s loyalty to his roots and his determination to protect what is his.

The chorus of the song serves as a reminder of That Mexican OT’s resilience and determination. With references to Jolly Ranchers and Codeine, pouring a 40, and the South Side of Texas, the artist implies his connection to the streets and his commitment to stay strong and hold his ground. He warns others that the “Kick Doe Click” will make him kick doors in, metaphorically symbolizing his unstoppable drive to succeed.

“Kick Doe Click” is a powerful song that not only showcases That Mexican OT’s lyrical prowess, but also offers a glimpse into his mindset and personal experiences. With its gritty verses and catchy chorus, the track leaves a lasting impression on anyone who listens to it.

In conclusion, “Kick Doe Click” by That Mexican OT is a compelling song that delves into the artist’s struggles, victories, and unwavering determination. It embodies the spirit of resilience and reflects the artist’s cultural identity. This track serves as a reminder that our experiences shape us and influence the art we create. If you haven’t listened to “Kick Doe Click” yet, I highly recommend giving it a chance and diving into the world of That Mexican OT’s music.
