John Cena gives off some serious Ken-ergy vibes in behind-the-scenes Barbie image

From years of body slamming muscular competition during his WWE days to serving as one of the brightest figures in modern-day DC, there’s not much that megastar John Cena won’t do at this point. Interestingly enough, the acclaimed performer continues to embrace that mentality on a daily basis, with Cena even agreeing to sign on for Greta Gerwig’s fantasy spectacle Barbie. And if you think you’ve seen it all from Cena, then your mind is about to be blown.
