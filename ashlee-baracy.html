Biography

Ashlee Baracy was born in Westland, MI on April 22, 1985. After being crowned Miss Michigan 2008, this Midwestern model was a Top Ten finalist in the 2009 Miss America pageant. She also worked as a traffic reporter for an NBC-affiliated station in Detroit, Michigan. She and Miss America 1988, Kaye Lani Rafko, both won the Miss Michigan title. After graduating from John Glenn High School in Westland, Michigan, she enrolled at the University of Michigan. Before becoming Miss Michigan, she held the 2003 title of Michigan’s Junior Miss.
