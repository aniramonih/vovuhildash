Who is Kene Holliday?

Kene Holliday is a well-known American actor who played Tyler Hudson in the court drama Matlock.

He is also recognized for his part in the ABC comedy Carter Country as Sgt. Curtis Baker.

Aside from that, his first significant film appearance was in Larry Cohen’s “See China and Die,” which starred Esther Rolle and Frank Converse.

Kene Holliday grew interested in acting and took a prominent role in the university’s yearly play. Holliday earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1971 and subsequently worked at the Folger Shakespeare Library in Washington, D.C.

Kene Holliday- Professional Life, Career
