In 2023, The Esteemed School Of Performing Arts Seoul (SOPA) Will Graduate K-pop Stars

The School of Performing Arts Seoul, or SOPA for short, is a renowned high school for the arts that was established in 1966 and is situated in Seoul’s Gung-dong neighborhood. This prestigious institution has produced a large number of South Korean celebrities, primarily K-pop idols, and many more are now enrolled.

Idols typically attend SOPA instead of a traditional high school due to their busy schedules. Numerous idols leave SOPA each year. The graduation ceremony for 2023 will reportedly occur on February 9. Let’s take a look at the K-pop stars on the 2023 SOPA’s STAR list who are anticipated to graduate from this esteemed institution this year.

Park Jeongwoo

He is renowned for his soulful voice TREASURE’s lead vocalist is Park Jeongwoo. In 2004, he was born in Iksan, South Korea. He went to Australia as an exchange student while in elementary school. In the approaching commencement ceremony, this 18-year-old idol, a graduate of SOPA’s Practical Music Department, will receive her diploma.

Jang Wonyoung

As the fourth generation’s “IT girl,” Jang Wonyoung is the lead vocalist of the K-pop girl group IVE and a former member of IZ*ONE. She was born in Seoul in 2004. On February 9, Wonyoung, a graduate of the Practical Music Department, will leave SOPA. She once went to Yonggang Middle School but later stopped going.

Kim Yeonkyu

A member of the rookie boy band ATBO is Kim Yeonkyu. He was a 2004 Seoul-native and former YG Entertainment trainee. Most idols attend school in the field of Practical Music. Lee JaeheeThe South Korean girl trio WEEEKLY includes Lee Jaehee. She attends the Theatre and Film Program at SOPA. She was a kid actress, thus she picked this department over Practical Music. Jaehee, who graduated from Daesong Middle School, was born in Gyeonggi-do, South Korea, in 2004.

Lee Yu Jeong

The vocalist and youngest member of the K-pop girl group LIGHTSUM are Lee Yu Jeong. She participated in Produce 48 as well. Yujeong, a Theatre & Film Department student, will graduate from SOPA on February 9. She was a former student of Bongwon Middle School. In 2004, she was born in Seoul.

Cho Wooju

Cho Wooju is a vocalist and songwriter for the Wuzo Entertainment-managed K-pop boy band BLITZERS. He was the youngest member of the group when he joined the world on January 21, 2004. On the list of SOPA’s STARs who are anticipated to graduate this year is the idol, who is 19 years old.
