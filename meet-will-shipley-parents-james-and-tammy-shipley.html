Meet Will Shipley Parents: James And Tammy Shipley


Will Shipley is a talented football running back, born on August 29, 2002, in North Carolina, United States. His elder brother, James, is also an athlete. Will initially attended NC State University before transferring to Clemson University. Despite both parents being NC State alumni, they are now supporting Will at Clemson.

Will Shipley’s football journey began at Weddington High School in North Carolina, where he showcased impressive statistics despite missing his senior year. Since joining the Clemson Tigers, his performance has been outstanding, earning him accolades such as being chosen first-team All-ACC as an all-purpose player.

Will Shipley’s parents, James and Tammy Shipley, play significant roles in his life. His father, James Sr., holds a master’s degree in Sports Management from NC State University and previously coached the Weddington Titans football team. His mother, Tammy, a graduate of NC State, prioritized education but also encouraged her sons’ athletic pursuits.

Tammy was thrilled when Will showed potential as a professional athlete, even though she highly values education. She has been supportive of his choice to pursue a business degree at Clemson University while excelling in football. Both parents hope for his success in college football and eventual advancement to the NFL.

His Brother James Is a Lacrosse Player

Will Shipley’s elder brother, James Jr., is a lacrosse player at the University of Pennsylvania. He also achieved remarkable success in high school, winning state titles in both football and lacrosse. Currently pursuing a Business degree, James continues to excel in sports at the collegiate level.
