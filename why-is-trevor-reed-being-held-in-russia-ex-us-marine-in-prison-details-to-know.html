Why Is Trevor Reed Being Held In Russia? Ex-U.S. Marine In Prison-Details To Know

Former U.S. Marine Trevor Reed has been sentenced to jail in Russia. Find the reason why he is locked up in prison. 

Trevor Reed, an American citizen, has been detained and jailed in Russia for more than two years now. He had a days-long hunger strike which ended as he has "lost a lot of weight" and is "very sick now."

As per the statement, he stopped eating food on November 3 at 6 p.m. Officially, he refused food on the morning of November 4 from prison. 

Reed resumed eating food the morning of November 9. In between the time, he only drank water. 

Two years back, he was sentenced to prison by a Moscow court.
