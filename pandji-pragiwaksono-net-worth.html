Born in Singapore, Pandji spent his childhood in Jakarta. His father was involved in the timber business, which sparked Pandji’s interest in the diversity and potential of Indonesia from a young age.

How does he view his role as a comedian?

He sees his role as dual: both as the jester with the ear of the king and as a speaker to the masses. His mission is to address social and political issues through humor, making him a significant figure in Indonesian comedy.

What inspired him to embark on a world tour?

His world tour, titled Juru Bicara (Spokesperson), aims to discuss current affairs, issues, and societal problems faced by Indonesians living abroad in an amusing manner. This tour reflects his personal approach to comedy, focusing more on social and political issues.
