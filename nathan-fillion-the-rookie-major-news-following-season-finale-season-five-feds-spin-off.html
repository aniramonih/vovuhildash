The Rookie fans celebrate huge news ahead of season five

What does this mean for the future of the show?

    The Rookie viewers were recently left overjoyed when they discovered the hit LAPD drama was being renewed for season five, and now there is a new update. 

    Following this season's final episode, fans have been delighted to discover the show's spin-off, , has been ordered to series.
