Shin Yuna is a South Korean singer, rapper, dancer, and TV personality who rose to prominence after debuting with the girl group, ITZY. She has served as the lead dancer, lead rapper, vocalist, visual, and maknae of the girl group whose other members are Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Hwang Ye-ji. Together, they have released a number of singles, EPs, and albums which include the likes of It’z Icy, It’z Summer, Cherry, Dalla Dalla, It’z Different, and Icy. Shin Yuna grew up in South Korea and also took great interest in a couple of sports like floorball. After being scouted by JYP Entertainment, she trained with the entertainment company for around 3 years before making her debut as a member of ITZY on February 12, 2019. Soon after its debut, ITZY started making headlines by getting ranked in top music charts and has also garnered millions of fans across the world as well as an impressive social media fan base with more than 3 million followers on Instagram, more than a million followers on Twitter, more than 800k followers on Facebook, and more than 2 million subscribers on YouTube. As a member of ITZY, Shin Yuna has made a big name in the music industry with her work and ITZY has also won a large number of awards and nominations.
