How rich is Emily Blunt: What is Her Net Worth

Emily Blunt is a British-American actress who rose to prominence with movies such as The Adjustment Bureau, Looper, Edge of Tomorrow among others. As of 2023, she is said to have an estimated net worth of US$80 million. The primary source of her income comes from her acting profession, promotions, and endorsement deals.

Who is Emily Blunt?

Еmіlу Оlіvіа Lеаh Вlunt popularly known as Emily Blunt was born on February 23, 1983 in the London Borough of Wandsworth in the United Kingdom. She is the second child of four children of her parents Joanna and Oliver Blunt. Her siblings are Felicity, Sebastian, and Susannah her siblings.

Emily Blunt’s from a very prominent family. Her father was a barrister while her paternal grandfather was Major General Peter Blunt, and one of her paternal uncles is Crispin Blunt, Conservative Member of Parliament for Reigate.

Blunt had a stuttering problem which started when she was seven years until she became 14. All credits was giving to her school teacher who help her manage through acting. For being able to manage and achieve success through this difficulty, she sat on the board of directors for the American Institute for Stuttering.

Emily Blunt attended Ibstock Place School in Roehamptori, south London. When she was 16, she attended Hurtwood House, a private college for performing arts programme. While there, she was discovered and signed by an agent.

Blunt made her professional debut on November 2001 starring in Peter Hall’s production of the play “The royal Family”. She played the role of Gwen, the granddaughter of Judi Dench’s Fanny Cavendish. The movie was a hit and was hailed by critic Tom Keatinge.

Blunt’s breakthrough came in 2006 with her starring roles in the television film Gideon’s Daughter and the comedy-drama film The Devil Wears Prada. The former won her a Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress and the latter earned her a nomination for the BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Supporting Role.

As her career progressed, she played more prominent roles. Other notable mentions are The Young Victoria, Salmon Fishing in the Yemen, The Adjustment Bureau, Into the Woods and Mary Poppins Returns.

For her brilliant performances, she has been nominated a number of times and she’s won the BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role, and SAG Award for Best Supporting Actress.

Emily Blunt is married to John Krasinski. The couple began dating in November 2008, got engaged in 2009 and later married on July 10, 2010. They are blessed with two children Hazel and Violet. They live in the Brooklyn Heights neighborhood of New York City.
