Who Is Adam Driver?

Adam Driver, the acclaimed American actor known for his intense performances, distinctive looks, and notable roles in projects like “Star Wars,” “Marriage Story,” and “BlacKkKlansman.” Born on November 19, 1983, in San Diego, California, to Joe and Nancy Wright, he has Dutch, English, German, Irish, and Scottish ancestry. Despite sharing the last name, Adam Driver is not related to Minnie Driver.

Is Adam Driver Related To Minnie Driver?
