Cookie Run Tower of Adventures Tier List Best Cookies in the Game

Are you looking for a tier list in Cookie Run Tower of Adventures? Here’s every cookie in the current beta test.

Overall, the best Cookie in CRToA is the Rye Cookie, with the Crushed Pepper coming close after. The best free Cookie is the Cherry Cookie.

Keep in mind that the game is only in beta version and more cookies will be added before release. However, for now, there are only six cookies available. Let’s check them in further detail.

Cookie Run Tower of Adventures Tier List

As of the current beta version, there are six cookies available in the game. Here are all Cookie Run Tower of Adventures cookies, ranked from best to worst:

  • S-tier: Rye Cookie
  • A-tier: Crushed Pepper Cookie
  • B-tier: Cherry Cookie, Angel Cookie, Twisted Donut Cookie
  • C-tier: GingerBrave

Overall, if you’re lucky enough to get your hands on any of the Epic Cookies, you’ll be on the right track.

Nevertheless, even Rare and Common Cookies can get the job done, especially since the story only includes some early-game levels.

The Angel Cookie and the Twisted Donut Cookie offer great stats all around and can be used early on, while GingerBrave is nothing special since it is the default cookie and provides less value compared to everything else.

Best Cookies in CRToA

Based on the tier list above, the best cookie in Cookie Run Tower of Adventures is the Rye Cookie. This is because her ultimate is extremely strong and she automatically reloads after dashing.

These make her a great choice for fast raids and will be one of the best cookies in the game overall when the game fully releases.

Secondarily, the Crushed Pepper Cookie is also a great option in terms of Epic Cookies, while the Cherry Cookie is the best Common option due to its insanely high AoE damage.

Wrapping up

To summarize this Cookie Run Tower of Adventures tier list, the best cookies in the game are first the Rye Cookie and second the Crushed Pepper Cookie.

For a free-to-play option, the Cherry Cookie is a great starter since it deals heavy AoE damage.
