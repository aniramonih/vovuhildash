Kristine Froseth is a model turned actress with a Norwegian heritage. She is renowned for her role as Veronica in Sierra Burgess Is a Loser, Ffion in Apostle, Jess in Junior, Shirley Blaney in Rebel In The Rye, and Nola Kellergan in The Truth About the Harry Quebert Affair. She has worked for brands like CHANEL, Miu Miu, Calvin Klein, Gucci, Armani Beauty, H&M, and Forever21. Kristine has also been featured in one of The Weeknd’s music videos and has over 1 million followers on Instagram.
