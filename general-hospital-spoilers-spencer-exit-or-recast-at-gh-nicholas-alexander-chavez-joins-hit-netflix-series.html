General Hospital Spoilers: Spencer Exit or Recast at GH Nicholas Alexander Chavez Joins Hit Netf

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that the show will need an exit strategy for Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Alexander Chavez). Deadline is reporting that Chavez is headed to Netflix for an amazing new gig, so let’s talk about what we know so far.

It’s obvious that Nicholas Alexander Chavez is ready to branch out and focus on bigger projects.
