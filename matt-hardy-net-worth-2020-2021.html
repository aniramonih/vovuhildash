Early Life

He is the son of Gilbert, and Ruby Moore Hardy and was born on 23 September 1974 in Cameron, North Carolina, United States. He has one younger brother Jeff Hardy, who is also a professional wrestler as well as his tag team partner. He lost his mother due to brain cancer when he was just 12 years old. Hardy went to Union Pines High School in North Carolina.

During his school time, he was used to playing baseball and football. He was a very good student, which leads him to be in a nominee list for “Morehead Award.” He majored in engineering from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. But Due to his father bad health, he dropped out and later continues his studies at Sandhills Community College in Pinehurst.
