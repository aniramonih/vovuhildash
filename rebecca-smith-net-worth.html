Rebecca Smith, a versatile violinist and violist, has carved a unique niche in the classical music world with her eclectic performances across various genres. Born and raised in the cultural hub of London, Rebecca’s musical journey is a blend of rich performances and educational achievements.

Summer Serenades at Scarborough

Every summer, the scenic town of Scarborough comes alive with the sound of Rebecca’s violin. As the sole violinist for the Scarborough Spa Orchestra, she performs a rigorous schedule of nine concerts each week. These performances are a testament to her stamina and dedication to her craft.
