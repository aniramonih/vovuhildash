Slash was first grown in his hometown for some time by his father only due to his mother’s work. Slash and his father later went to Los Angeles, joining Slash’s mother, but his parents separated in 1974. He has one brother, and his name is Albion Hudson. Slash lived with his mother following his parent’s separation. During that time, he used to go with his mother to work. Career

Following that, Slash and Steven Adler joined a band named “Hollywood Rose” for some time and then tried to join two bands but failed, and their names are Black Sheep and Poison. Axl Rose and Izzy Stradlin asked Slash to join them in “Guns N’ Roses.”

His starting days with the Guns N’ Roses were about playing and performing in clubs such as Troubadour, Roxy Theatre and Whisky a Go-Go. Slash released his self-titled studio album in 2010, with the participation of Guns N’ Roses debut album members. His latest work was an album along with Myles Kennedy and The Conspirators titled “World on Fire,” and it was a hit as it successfully ranked on top charts.
