Krista Allen Net Worth

Krista Allen is an American actress with a net worth of $4 million dollars. She is best known for her work on Days of Our Lives and Baywatch Hawaii. Allen has appeared in various films such as Liar Liar, Confessions of a Dangerous Mind, Anger Management, and The Final Destination. She is known for her ability to take on various character traits and cross between genres.

Allen began her acting career in 1994 with a role in Emmanuel in Space, an erotic-themed made-for-TV movie. She then went on to take additional roles for television and movies, becoming more well-known among the public. Today, Allen has regular recurring and guest roles on TV shows.

Aside from acting, Allen follows a vegan lifestyle, which she writes about in her blog, Veggie Boom Boom. In 2009, she married Iranian rapper Mams Taylor, but the couple filed for separation in 2011 and divorced in February 2012.
