Who is Jonathan Majors Current Girlfriend? Grace Jabbari and Meagan Good Explored

Who is Jonathan Major’s Current Girlfriend and who are  Grace Jabbari and Meagan Good?

Jonathan Majors’ past and present partners present a stark contrast during the trial.

One accuses him of assault, while the other enters the courtroom hand in hand with him.

Marvel star Jonathan Majors is currently on trial, facing accusations of assault from his ex-girlfriend, Grace Jabbari.

Throughout the courtroom proceedings, he has been accompanied by his new girlfriend, Meagan Good.

Majors is charged with two counts of harassment and two counts of assault, stemming from an alleged incident in March 2023 in New York City.

The accusations include slapping Jabbari, throwing her into a car, twisting her arm, and causing injury to her hand. Despite the charges, Majors has pleaded not guilty to all counts.

The trial has seen extensive deliberation by jurors, spanning nearly three days.

Testimonies from key witnesses, including Jabbari, her friend and choreographer, and the driver of the private vehicle, have been presented during the proceedings.

Who is Grace Jabbari?

Grace Jabbari, a 30-year-old British dancer, is the ex-girlfriend of Jonathan Majors, and their relationship spanned two years.

As a dancer, Jabbari has been featured in films such as Barbie, Mission Impossible: Dead Reckoning Part One, and Coldplay’s “Cry Cry Cry” music video, as indicated by her IMDB profile.

During her testimony in court, Jabbari informed jurors that her romance with Majors commenced in August 2021 after they met on the set of Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania. She worked as a movement director, while Majors played the role of the movie’s villain.

Jabbari recounted that Majors’s hair stylist discreetly passed her his number, and he later approached her, asking, “Am I going to see you later?” She described their relationship as progressing rapidly, with Majors expressing his feelings early on and even writing poetry for her.

However, by December, Jabbari noticed a change in their dynamic.

When she mentioned her ex, Majors allegedly responded with anger, marking the first time she felt scared of him.

Throughout the trial, multiple incidents beyond the one Major is being tried for came to light, revealing what the prosecutor described as a “manipulative pattern” of abuse.

Text messages presented in court disclosed an episode in September 2022 in London, where Majors seemed to admit to causing injury to Jabbari’s head. He then reportedly threatened to commit suicide if she sought medical attention, as revealed in the texts.

Who is Meagan Good?

Meagan Good, aged 42, is an actress and model, currently serving as the girlfriend of Jonathan Majors.

With a presence in both television shows and movies, Good has credits in productions like Harlem, Shazam! Fury of the Gods, and Think Like a Man, as documented on her IMDB page.

The couple began their relationship in May 2023, providing mutual support during the time when Majors faced allegations, according to People.

Despite speculations arising from Majors referring to Good as “the missus” in a September speech, sources clarified to People that they are still in the dating phase.

Photographs captured during the trial depict the couple entering the courtroom hand in hand. Additionally, during the closing remarks of Majors’ defense attorney, an emotional Good was seen wiping tears from her eyes.
