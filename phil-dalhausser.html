Who is Phil Dalhausser:

Phil Dalhausser is a famous Volleyball Player. He was born on January 26, 1980 and his birthplace is Switzerland. Phil is also well known as, Decorated men’s beach volleyball player who took home the gold medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics. He is also a multiple-time winner on the Men’s FIVB World Tour and was named the Most Outstanding player on the tour in 2008 and 2010.

Phil is originated from Switzerland. He won five straight AVP Tour titles with partner Todd Rogers.
