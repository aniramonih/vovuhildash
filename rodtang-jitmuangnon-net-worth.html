Rodtang Jitmuangnon Net Worth

Rodtang Jitmuangnon ( born July 23, 1997) is a Thai Muay Thai fighter. He currently competes in the ONE Championship and is the reigning ONE Flyweight Muay Thai World Champion. Jitmuangnon is also a former Omnoi Stadium 130lb Champion and MAX Muay Thai 125lb Champion.

Rodtang had his first Muay Thai fight at the age of 8 to make money and help his family. At 14, he moved to Bangkok to join the Jitmuangnon gym.

Rodtang Jitmuangnon Net Worth

Rodtang Jitmuangnon has a net worth of $3.2 million, He is unarguably a true superstar of the Muay Thai community in both Thailand and overseas for he has a very entertaining fighting style. Now Rodtang is a current champion of ONE Championship, where he receives 1.5 million baht per fight at the promotion while earning 200,000 to 250,000 baht per fight in Thailand.

On January 2, 2016, Rodtang won his first Muay Thai title when he defeated Petchprakan Kor. Klanbut by a decision to claim the MAX Muay Thai 125 lb Championship. On February 6, 2016, he successfully defended his MAX 125 lb title belt against Petchprakan, winning by decision once again.
