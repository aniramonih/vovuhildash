Who is Este Haim:

Este Haim is a famous Rock Singer. She was born on March 14, 1986 and her birthplace is Los Angeles, CA. Este is also well known as, Singer and bassist with the Los Angeles based indie rock band Haim alongside her sisters Alana and Danielle Haim. She is known for her comical “bass face” and showmanship on stage.

Este is originated from United States. She and Haim have opened for such artists as Kesha and Edward Sharpe and the Magnetic Zeros.
