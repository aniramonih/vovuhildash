Jrgen Klopp Net Worth | Celebrity Net Worth

Jurgen Klopp net worth and salary: Jurgen Klopp is a German professional soccer manager and former professional soccer player who has a net worth of $50 million. Jurgen Klopp was born in Stuttgart, West Germany in June 1967. He was a striker and defender who played his youth career for SV Glatten and TuS Ergenzingen. Klopp played his entire senior career for FSV Mainz 05 where he scored 52 goals in 337 appearances from 1990 to 2001. He then became the manager of FSV Mainz 05 from 2001 to 2008. From 2008 to 2015 he managed Borussia Dortmund. Klopp helped lead Borussia Dortmund to win the Bundesliga in 2011 and 2012 as well as the DFB-Pokal in 2012 and the DFL-Supercup in 2008, 2013, and 2014.

He was named German Football Manager of the Year in 2011 and 2012. He has had advertising deals with companies such as Puma. He was named head coach of Liverpool in 2015. His annual salary at Borussia was $6 million. At Liverpool he earns $8 million base salary. Under Klopp, Liverpool won the 2019 UEFA Championship.
