Early Life & Biography

John Morgan was born in Lexington, Kentucky, U.S., the eldest of five children. When Morgan was 14, his family moved to Winter Park in Florida. John Morgan was a student at the University of Florida. That is where he pursued his Bachelor of Arts and Juris Doctor degrees.

When John Morgan was a 2nd-year student at the University of Florida in 1976, his younger brother, Tim, was paralyzed while making a rescue dive as a lifeguard at Walt Disney World. When the elder Morgan enrolled in law school four years later, his brother’s battle led him to one legal speciality: personal injury.
