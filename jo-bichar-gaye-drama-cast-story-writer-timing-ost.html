Jo Bichar Gaye Drama Cast, Story, Writer, Timing, OST

Jo Bichar Gaye is a 2021 historical drama aired on Geo Entertainment with lead cast Maya Ali, Wahaj Ali, and Talha Chahour. In 2021, Geo entertainment produced many hit dramas with unique stories. This serial is an amazing addition to the success journey of Geo Entertainment. The drama is produced by H2 Films and penned down by Col Z. I. Farrukh.

Jo Bichar Gaye drama story revolves around the history of Pakistan, Bangladesh, and India. The lead roles are performed by talented Maya Ali and Wahaj Ali. They will appear first time as an on-screen couple and their fans are very excited to see them together. Talha Chahour is a rising star of the Pakistan drama industry. He is also performing the lead role in this serial. The drama story is full of action, suspense, and emotions.
