Charlotte Flair &amp; Andrades Social Media Activity Fuels Breakup Rumors

Charlotte Flair and Andrade started dating publicly in early 2019. Their relationship was confirmed when they started sharing photos together on social media and making appearances as a couple at various events. They were spotted together all the time while Andrade was with WWE. The two tied the knot this year, but there might be issues already.

Even after Andrade departed from WWE, he was seen with Charlotte Flair at the WWE Hall of Fame. They were quite happy, and gave no appearance that their engagement would come to any end, except through marriage, and certainly not in a divorce.

Charlotte Flair even said in the past that she feels more confident after marrying Andrade El Idolo. The two seemingly have a storybook romance, as they are both focused on their careers with different companies.

The two even traded nods to each other after Andrade’s AEW return. He finished off match with a Figure 8 and Charlotte Flair then did a tranquilo pose during her own match on WWE television. Now, it appears that the two might not be a couple anymore.

Charlotte Flair and Andrade unfollowed each other on social media. There is no public word on their relationship status in an official sense, but that won’t stop fans from speculating that there is trouble in paradise.

Ric Flair also unfollowed Andrade on social media. Charlotte Flair took the diamond ring emoji off her bio as well.

We will keep our eye on this story, right here at Ringside News. There could be a lot more to unpack, and a messy breakup is not what anyone wants for Charlotte Flair and Andrade El Idolo.
