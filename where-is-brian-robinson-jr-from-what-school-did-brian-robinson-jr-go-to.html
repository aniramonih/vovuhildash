Who Is Brian Robinson Jr?

Brian Robinson Jr. is a footballer who plays as running back for the Washington Commanders of the National Football League (NFL).

Born on March 22, 1999, in Alabama, Brian attended Hillcrest High School, where he recorded 990 rushing yards and 18 touchdowns as a senior.

He moved on to play football at the University of Alabama, winning two national championships.

In his freshman year, Brian recorded 165 rushing yards and two touchdowns. As a sophomore, he rushed 272 yards and two touchdowns on 63 carries.

Brian was named the most valuable player of the year at the 2021 Cotton Bowl Classic, following a successful bowl game.

For five seasons, Robinson played with the Crimson Tide, and recorded 483 rushing yards and six touchdowns on 91 carries. He averaged a total of 5.3 yards per carry.

In 2022, the Washington Commanders drafted Brian as 98th pick, in the third round of the NFL Draft for the year, and made him sign a four-year rookie contract with them.
