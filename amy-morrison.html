Pioneering the Beauty Industry

Amy Morrison is a trailblazing entrepreneur who has left an indelible mark on the beauty industry. She gained recognition with her favourite beauty blog, “Into The Gloss”, before launching Glossier in 2014.

Glossier quickly gained a loyal following for its minimalist aesthetic and affordable prices. In 2017, Glossier opened its first brick-and-mortar store, and in 2019, the brand achieved unicorn status with a valuation of over $1 billion. Morrison’s entrepreneurial prowess earned her a spot on Forbes’ “America’s Richest Self-Made Women” list.

In 2021, Morrison stepped down as CEO of Glossier but remained on the board of directors while also launching “Somewhere Good Ventures”, a venture capital fund focused on empowering underrepresented founders in the beauty and wellness industries. Morrison’s visionary leadership and commitment to inclusivity have made her a respected figure in the beauty world and beyond.
