What is Ying Yang Twins' Net Worth?

The Ying Yang Twins are an American hip hop due who have a net worth of $100 thousand dollars. The Ying Yang Twins consist of D-Roc, born De'Angelo Holmes in 1979, and Kaine, born Eric Jackson in 1978. They made their debut with 2000's "Whistle While You Twurk", which climbed to the top 20 on the hip hop charts, and released their first album that same year. Their second album, "Alley: The Return of the Ying Yang Twins", gained popularity in the southern U.S. where the duo was based. In 2002, they were featured on Lil John's "Get Low", which was wildly successful commercially, and their next album, "Me & My Brother", went platinum.

The Ying Yang Twins reached the height of their popularity in the mid-2000s with "U.S.A. (United State of Atlanta)" and "Chemically Imbalanced", with singles that featured Wyclef Jean. Since 2000, the duo has released seven studio albums, two remix albums and three compilations, including "Legendary Status: Ying Yang Twins Greatest Hits" which features three top ten singles. They have appeared on MTV's "Cribs" and in the 2004 film "Soul Plane". Their single "Halftime (Stand Up and Get Crunk)" is routinely featured in NFL stadiums around the country and is particularly popular with the New Orleans Saints.
