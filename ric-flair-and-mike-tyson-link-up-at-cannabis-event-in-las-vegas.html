Ric Flair and Mike Tyson Link Up at Cannabis Event in Las Vegas

Wrestling icon Ric Flair and boxing legend Mike Tyson have joined forces in the heart of Las Vegas, not for a showdown in the ring, but for a celebration of a different kind: cannabis.

The unexpected duo made waves on social media as Flair took to Twitter to share footage of their rendezvous, showcasing them indulging in joints amidst the vibrant lights of Sin City. The caption accompanying the video simply stated, “The Kings Of Cannabis Taking Over Las Vegas! WOOOOO!”

Their meeting took place at the CHAMPS Trade Shows, a renowned event in the cannabis industry, which is scheduled from February 14th to 17th, 2024. This annual gathering serves as a platform for cannabis enthusiasts, businesses, and industry professionals to come together, exchange ideas, and showcase the latest innovations in the burgeoning cannabis market.

For wrestling fans, seeing Ric Flair in the midst of a cannabis convention might come as a surprise. However, Flair’s venture into the cannabis world is not entirely new. The wrestling legend has been vocal about his support for cannabis legalization and its potential benefits, both for medicinal and recreational use.

In recent years, he has embraced the cannabis culture, even partnering with companies in the industry to launch his own line of cannabis products.
