    A boogie with hoodie facts

  • His early teens were smooth and quiet as he rarely got into trouble because of his shy and secretive nature. However, the difficult years began after he got into high school, when he started skipping classes and selling drugs.
  • He was arrested many times in New York for selling marijuana and cannabis. When he turned 16, his parents decided to send him to Florida hoping he wouldn’t get into trouble there. Even in Florida, he refused to reform and was eventually placed under house arrest. However, this turned out to be a blessing for him as he had plenty of free time to work on his music.
  • After graduating from high school, he decided to focus on his music career and took on a few jobs, including pizza deliveries and construction work, to make ends meet.
  • It took a long time for him to complete his debut on Limited He struggled with rap flow, which led him to hire a vocal coach to get his vocal technique right. He finally released the single at the age of 19.
  • In 2015 he decided to return to New York to pursue his music career full-time.
  • His debut mixtape, Artist was inspired by a messy romantic relationship. He was inspired by his suffering to write his popular song. Still thinking about you .
  • In 2015 he co-founded his Highbridge label with fellow rapper Quincy “QP” Acheampong. They also created a home studio.
  • “A” in his rap name comes from his first name “Artist”, Boogie comes from his childhood neighborhood in the Bronx, and the hoodie suffix comes from his love of wearing hoodies.
  • In June 2014 he released his first soundtrack on SoundCloud. It was a freestyle called 0 to 100 .
