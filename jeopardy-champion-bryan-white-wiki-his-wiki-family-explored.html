Jeopardy Champion Bryan White Wiki: His Wiki &amp; Family Explored

A trivia lover, Bryan White made his Jeopardy debut on June 28 episode this season. He is now a new two-day champion of the show with total winnings of $32,007.

Who is Jeopardy champion Bryan White and what is his job? Know about his wiki, age, job, married life, family, and Jeopardy journey here.

Bryan White is a new Jeopardy champion who has now won two episodes of the show. He will return as a champion on June 30 episode this Friday.

Bryan White Is A Two-Day Jeopardy Champion

White made his Jeopardy debut on the 28 June episode of the 39th season of the longest-running quiz game show, Jeopardy! There, he faced previous episode champion Chris Ban and Vienna, Virginia-based lawyer Vic Goel.


Chris is an oral and facial surgeon from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania who returned as a champion on the show. However, Bryan ended up being a champion with a score of $20,130.

Likewise, he continued his winning streak on June 29 episode where he faced Alicia Korenman is an Assistant Director from Durham, North Carolina, and Tenysa Santiago is an American sign language and English interpreter from Berkeley, California. He ended up being a champion with a final score of $11,877.


Bryan is a two-day champion with total winnings of $32,007. He will return as a champion on Friday’s episode on 30 June 2023.

Bryan White Wiki, Age & Education

Born on 16 May 1982, White has reached 41 years old now. He hails from Santa Maria, California and is American by nationality.

Moreover, he attended Valley Christian Academy in his hometown and graduated in the year 2000. He was also a star Bible Quiz team member during his days at school.

White obtained a Master of Arts in Drama from San Francisco State University in 2011. Similarly, he earned a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Theatre Literature, History, and Criticism from the University of California San Diego in 2019.

Bryan White Wife & Daughter: Inside His Married Life

White has been married to Paula White for more than a decade. His wife Paula hails from New Rockford, North Dakota, and both the couple works at the same company.

Paula is the Vice President of CCA Operations at Calpine Energy Solutions, LLC. She has been serving in the company for nearly two decades, joining in 2004.


The duo is parenting a daughter named Emma White, born in January 2014. He now resides with his wife and nine-year-old daughter in Santa Maria, California.

Bryan White Is A Regulatory Compliance Analyst By Job

White now serves as a Senior Regulatory Compliance Analyst at Calpine Energy Solutions, LLC. He has been holding the job at the energy company for over a decade. He joined the company as an Enrollments Associate in 2011.

Previously, he worked as a Regulatory Compliance Analyst at San Diego-based Noble Americas Energy Solutions, LLC from 2008 to 2009. Likewise, he worked at Sempra Energy Solutions LLC as an analyst from 2005 to 2008.

Bryan began his profession as an Energy Rebate Processor at San Diego Gas & Electric. He served there for several months in 2005.
