The Identity of Tom Austen Wife Is Complicated After His Rumored Lover Marry Someone Else

Like his younger brother, Freddy Carter, who is currently experiencing a surge in popularity thanks to his performance in the Shadow and Bone series, Tom Austen is also an up-and-coming actor best known for his role as Jasper Frost in The Royals!

Does Tom Austen Have A Wife?

Tom Austen is notoriously private regarding his personal life, so few people know whether the actor is off the market.

However, in 2019 Tom attended the 25th Annual Daisy Ball with fellow actress Poppy Corby-Tuech. That would be the second ball they attended together after being spotted at the same event a year prior.
