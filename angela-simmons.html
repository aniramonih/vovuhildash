Angela Simmons Baby Daddy, Who Is Angela Simmons Husband?

But before Angela Simmons got married, she fell under a rough spell. Angela Simmons broke her engagement with Sutton in December of 2017, and every aspect of her starting a married life with Sutton as husband fell apart. Now Angela Simmons is single and a single mother caring for her baby.

Angela Simmons had kept quiet about her fall of marriage for some time, until in season 4 of ‘Growing Up Hip-Hop’ she finally confessed the suffering she went through, trying to have a have a family and caring for her baby without his daddy.
