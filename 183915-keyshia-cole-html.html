Keyshia Cole's Oldest Son Daniel Holds Baby Brother Tobias in New Photo That Shows Their Resemblance

Keyshia Cole has shared a new photo of her sons hanging out together, and it's too cute for words. The singer only recently shared the first photo of her infant son, and it appears fans can't get enough.

Keyshia, 38, has shared several pictures of her sons, Daniel "DJ" Gibson Jr. and Tobias Khale, but this will be the first time she captured both brothers together, showing off their dynamic and uncanny resemblance.

Keyshia Cole at the Soul Train Awards on Nov. 17, 2019 in Las Vegas | Photo: Getty Images

Nine-year-old DJ, who underwent minor surgery in May, looked every inch the proud big brother as he cuddled Tobias, four months. The look on his face was one of pure bliss as he secured the younger guy.

Keyshia and her baby daddy, Niko Khale, went public with their relationship in early 2018 and stayed strong despite criticism

While the big brother sported a grey t-shirt and black pants, Tobias had on a black onesie that featured a hood worn over his jet-black curls. Both boys were smiling, a sign that they enjoyed each other's company.

Fans couldn't hide their admiration for Keyshia's boys in the comment section, filling up the space with sweet words and tons of related emojis.

"They are both so adorable," gushed one Instagram user who also gave the boys virtual hugs.

"Adorable pic cutiest (sic) brothers," echoed another.

While Tobias made his world debut in August, Keyshia did not share a photo of the infant until last month after the premiere of her new BET reality show, "Keyshia Cole: My New Life."

The "Heaven Sent" singer posted a series of images from a photoshoot where Tobias was dressed in knit caps and shorts. The little man was asleep in some shots while he giggled happily in others.

In the caption of that post, Keyshia disclosed some details about her baby's personality, calling him "the sweetest baby." She added:

"He's such a little blessing, and I’m completely in love with him! The smiles he gives EVERY SINGLE Time you smile at him, Brightens the worst of days. So happy [Daniel Gibson Jr.] has a baby brother now."

Keyshia and her baby daddy, Niko Khale, went public with their relationship in early 2018 and stayed strong despite criticism over their age gap – Keyshia is 14 years older than her music artist beau.

Last month, the California native got defensive after comedian Nick Cannon referred to her as Niko's elder during an interview. Nick is no stranger to being in a relationship with an older woman. He was married to Mariah Carey, who is ten years his senior, for many years.

"To be in a relationship and to start a family with someone like this, I mean, I've been in that situation before where I stepped into royalty, to an elder, and she guided me," Nick said to Keyshia and Niko. "You're an elder."

"I am not a [expletive] elder," she retorted.

Keyshia has been in the process of divorcing her estranged husband, former NBA player Daniel Gibson Sr., since 2017. Meanwhile, it appears the singer is having the time of her life with Niko and her two sons.
