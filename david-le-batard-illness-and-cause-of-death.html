David Le Batard Illness and Cause of Death

The answer is yes. He was suffering from a prolonged undisclosed illness. David’s older brother Dan Le Batard revealed his death.

David passed away at the age of 50. He got diagnosed with an illness about a year ago but he never revealed his illness in the media. His brother said,

“my best friend for over 50 years,” as “inconceivable.”
