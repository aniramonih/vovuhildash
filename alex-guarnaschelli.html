Biography

Alex Guarnaschelli was born in New York City, NY on June 20, 1972. Celebrity chef and Iron Chef seen on the Food Network shows Chopped, Iron Chef America, All Star Family Cook-off, and The Best Thing I Ever Ate who also hosted Alex’s Day Off and The Cooking Loft. She is the executive chef at Butter restaurant in NYC and previously held that role at the award-winning The Darby. She defeated Amanda Freitag in the final showdown of season 5’s The Next Iron Chef to be crowned an Iron Chef.
