What We Know About James Kennedy's New Girlfriend Ally Lewber

DJ James Kennedy first alerted the masses that he had a new leading lady by the name of Ally Lewber, when he posted a series of photos of the pair enjoying a vacation in Tulum, Mexico. "Only the most magical trip I've ever experienced in my entire life, here's a little slide show of todays adventure.... Share the love people! and the positivity! cause life's too short" he penned in the caption. 

Following the Instagram debut, however, James appeared on an episode of Lala Kent's podcast, "Give Them Lala" where he provided a few more deets about Lewber, whom he met in January while at a concert featuring "Tom Sandoval and the Most Extras." But as for whether or not he planned to have her make a cameo on "Vanderpump Rules," James maintained that he wasn't so sure. "I do like this girl, I really do," he confessed. "With [Lewber], I'm taking my time. She gets to choose what she wants to do with that whole situation, and then for me, it's like, I have to decide as well. What I do know is I'm going to be doing me and I'm going to be f**king enjoying myself. Truly. Because honestly, after Raquel left, now it's like I don't have anyone to impress or please except for myself ... My heart cannot break anymore ... I don't care what anyone thinks or what everyone wants ... No one has control over me anymore," he declared.
