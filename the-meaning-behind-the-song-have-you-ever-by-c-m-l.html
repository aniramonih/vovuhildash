Have You Ever by C.M.L. is a powerful rap song that delves deep into the struggles and experiences faced by the artist. Released on February 4, 2020, as a part of the album Heavyweight Hank, this track reflects the harsh realities of life in the streets from a deeply personal perspective.

The lyrics of “Have You Ever” touch upon a wide range of intense emotions and experiences. C.M.L. paints a vivid picture of a life filled with both triumphs and tribulations. The song explores the artist’s journey through difficult circumstances, including being crossed, dealing with loss, engaging in criminal activities, and facing the consequences of their actions. C.M.L. also shares the pain of witnessing the struggles of loved ones, such as their parents’ battle with addiction.

What makes this song even more compelling is the raw honesty with which C.M.L. presents their experiences. It’s evident that the lyrics are deeply personal and drawn from the artist’s own life. The song resonates with listeners who have experienced similar hardships, allowing them to find solace and connection in the shared emotions expressed through music.

A Personal Connection

When I first listened to “Have You Ever,” I was immediately struck by the raw authenticity of the lyrics. The song not only exposes the realities of life on the streets but also captures the resilience and strength it takes to overcome adversity. As someone who has witnessed the struggles of loved ones caught in the cycle of addiction and crime, the lyrics hit close to home.

C.M.L.’s words bring back memories of the challenges faced by those I care about, and the song serves as a reminder of the importance of empathy and understanding. Life in these circumstances is far from glamorous, as the lyrics clearly depict. It’s a constant battle for survival, where loyalty is rare and trust is easily broken.

With lines like “Your heart ever turned black?” and “Have you ever caught a case with a rat,” C.M.L. exposes the dark realities of betrayal and emotional turmoil. These experiences leave lasting scars, challenging one’s ability to trust others and to find hope in an unforgiving world.

Despite the hardships conveyed in the song, “Have You Ever” also embodies a message of perseverance and resilience. C.M.L.’s lyrics speak to the ability to bounce back after hitting rock bottom, to keep pushing forward even when the odds are stacked against you. It’s a reminder that no matter how tough life gets, there is always a glimmer of hope and strength within us.

In conclusion, “Have You Ever” by C.M.L. is a poignant rap song that offers an unfiltered glimpse into the struggles and triumphs of the artist’s life. Through honest and introspective lyrics, C.M.L. invites listeners to reflect on their own experiences and find solace in shared emotions. The song serves as a reminder of the importance of empathy and resilience in navigating the challenges life throws our way.
