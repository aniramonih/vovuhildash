What is Marian Rivera's Net Worth?

Marian Rivera is a Spanish-born Filipina model and actress who has a net worth of $5 million. Marian Rivera was born in Madrid, Spain in August 1984. At an early age she modeled for SkinWhite lotion and Sky Flakes biscuits. She starred in afternoon TV soap operas on the GMA Network. Rivera starred in the television series Darna from 2009 to 2010. In 2010 she starred as Jenny Cruz in the TV series Endless Love. Rivera starred in the TV series Bampirella in 2011 and starred as Amaya in the series Amaya from 2011 to 2012.

In 2012 she starred as Sharina in the TV series My Beloved. She has also starred in the films Tarot, My Bestfriend's Girlfriend, You to Me Are Everything, and Panday 2. As a singer Rivera released her debut studio album Marian Rivera Dance Hits in 2008 and her second album Retro Crazy in 2009. Her single "Sabay Sabay Tayo" was released in 2009. In 2008 and 2013 Rivera was named FHM Magazine's Sexiest Woman of the World. Marian's longtime dance partner Dingdong Dantes proposed to her during a live airing of her dance program in August 2014 and the couple was married in December 2014.
