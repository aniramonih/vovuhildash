PUSHA T, WIFE, AND SON POSE IN SWEET FAMILY PHOTO

Pusha T and his wife, Virginia Williams, pose in sweet family photo with their son Nigel. Shot by photographer Malik Emmanuel, the picture shows the couple posing on a couch while Nigel sits on his dad’s lap.

The picture has received thousands of likes and hundreds of comments.

“Beautiful family,” one commenter said.

“He’s a perfect mix of both your faces,” another said.

In an earlier candid that Virginia posted on her Instagram last week, Nigel can be seen gazing at the camera with one  of his fingers placed strategically under his chin.

“I have a problem on my hands”, Virginia captioned of the photo.

Pusha T and Virginia Williams welcomed their son in June. Virginia shared an Instagram post that revealed the date Nigel was born as well as her son’s weight and height upon arrival.

Nigel’s arrival in June came after Pusha T announced his wife’s expectancy in December 2019. “Happy holidays,” Pusha said in reference to the Christmas season. “Baby T coming Spring 2020,” the rapper shared. Virginia also announced her pregnancy via Instagram.

“Finally landed my dream job,” Pusha T’s wife wrote online. “World, make way for BABY THORNTON! Arriving Gemini season 2020. We are over the moon.”

Nigel is Pusha T and Virginia Williams’ only child together.
