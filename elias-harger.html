Biography TimeLine

Elias Harger is an American actor known for his role as Max Fuller on the popular Netflix series “Fuller House.” Born on October 22, 2007, in Denver, Colorado, Harger began acting young. Here is a timeline of the significant events in his life:

  • 2014: Harger began attending acting classes at a local theatre in Denver, Colorado, at seven.
  • 2015: Harger made his acting debut in the short film “Pottersville”, playing the role of Bartender’s Son.
  • 2016: Harger landed his breakthrough role as Max Fuller on the Netflix original “Fuller House.”
  • 2017: Harger made his feature film debut in the drama film “Don’t Come Back from the Moon,” playing the role of Peter.
  • 2018: Harger reprised his role as Max Fuller for the fourth and final season of “Fuller House.”
  • 2020: Harger appeared in the independent film “Abe,” playing the role of Leo.
  • 2021: Harger landed a voice role in the animated movie “The Loud House Movie,” playing the character of Lincoln Loud.
