Maggie Smith is a British actress who has had an illustrious career over the course of which she has appeared in more than 60 movies and more than 70 plays. She is widely considered as one of the greatest actresses of her era having won 2 Academy Awards, a Tony Award, 4 Primetime Emmy Awards, 3 Golden Globe Awards, 5 SAG (Screen Actors Guild) Awards, 5 BAFTA Film Awards, and The Laurence Olivier Award for ‘Society of London Theatre Special Award’ in 2010. She was made a ‘Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire’ (DBE) in the 1990 New Year Honours by Queen Elizabeth II, for her contribution to performing arts; and a ‘Companion of Honour’ in 2014 for her services to drama.
