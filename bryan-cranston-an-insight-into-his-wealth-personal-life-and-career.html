Bryan Cranston: An Insight into His Wealth, Personal Life, and Career


Learn about Bryan Cranston and his personal life, including his net worth, age, wife, family, and biography.

Bryan Lee Cranston, a prominent figure in Hollywood, is recognized for his contributions as a director, producer, screenwriter, and actor. He is best known for portraying Walter White in Breaking Bad and Dr. Tim in Seinfeld on NBC.

Biography & Profile Summary

Bryan Cranston, born on 7th March 1956, is 67 Years Old as of 2023. He hails from a well-established Christian family in Los Angeles, California. Being of American nationality, he identifies with the Christian faith.

Cranston attended Canoga Park High School and later pursued his education at Los Angeles Valley College.

His passion for acting and extracurricular activities overshadowed his interest in academics since childhood.

Bryan Cranston Family, Wife & Relationships

Bryan Cranston’s father, Joe Cranston, and mother, Peggy Sell, both have ties to the entertainment industry. He has two siblings, Kyle Edward Cranston and Amy Cranston.

Maritally, Cranston was first married to Mickey Middleton in 1977 until their divorce in 1982. Later, in 1989, he tied the knot with Robin Dearden with whom he has a daughter, Taylor Dearden. Additionally, he was in a relationship with Mickey Middleton prior to his marriage to Dearden.

Bryan Cranston Height & Weight

Cranston stands at 5 feet 10 inches and weighs around 76 Kg. He possesses a charismatic personality, dark brown hair, and captivating dark brown eyes.

Bryan Cranston Career

Cranston embarked on his career as a writer in 1980. He made his debut appearance on the TV series To Race the Wind, followed by his role in the film The Call of the Wild: Howl Buck in 1981. Subsequently, he featured in various other TV shows and films.

Bryan Cranston Net Worth

As of 2023, Bryan Cranston’s net worth is estimated to be $40 million.
