Pre-Orders Now Available For Wave 2 Fanaticos Action Figures From Masked Republic

A look at brand new lucha libre action figures from Masked Republic.

In the second wave of Fanaticos action figures from Masked Republic and Boss Fight Studio, Ultimo Dragon, Vampiro, and Hijo del Perro Aguayo get their most articulated figures in history. Also, IMPACT Wrestling’s Black Taurus gets his first figure.

From Masked Republic:

Ultimo Dragon, Vampiro and Hijo del Perro Aguayo get their most articulated figures in history and Black Taurus makes his action figure debut.

With the first Legends of Lucha Libre Collectors’ Series figures of Penta Zero M and Rey Fenix sold out months ago and Fanaticos Series figures of Juventud Guerrera, Taya Valkyrie and the Lucha Brothers now available online and in select shops throughout the U.S. and U.K., Masked Republic and Boss Fight Studio have now opened pre-orders for Wave 2 Fanaticos.

With an average of 25 points of articulation (10 more points of articulation than WWE Basic figures) and highly detailed decoration (just check out Ultimo Dragon’s mask and chest piece) at $22.99 SRP there is no better value for quality on the market.

Why pre-order directly from Boss Fight Studio?

  • Guaranteed to receive the product.  With previous figures being sold out, this is your best way to guarantee you don’t miss out.
  • Receive it earliest – Other retailers may or may not ship the product at the same time, but all BFS pre-orders will ship within days of the figures arriving at the warehouse.

    • Fanaticos Wave 2 will ship in early 2023.

    Pre-order now at BossFightShop.com.
