Taylor Hawkins cause of death, age, Foo Fighters Drummer's family, net worth

American musician and drummer Taylor Hawkins has passed away. His death was reported on Friday, March 25, 2022, at the age of 50.

Taylor Hawkins was a member of the famous American band Foo Fighters. He joined the band in 1997 after two years as Alanis Morissette’s drummer and was a key element of the Foo Fighters’ sound and image, Variety reports.

He had the seemingly thankless task of playing drums behind Foos frontman Dave Grohl, who is one of the greatest drummers in rock history.

Hawkins filled that role with aplomb, bringing his own hard-hitting, time-juggling style to the band’s straight-ahead rock sound without trying to emulate Grohl.

He was also a strong singer and frequently took the mic during the band’s concerts and on B-sides, often on cover versions (such as a 2008 team-up with Led Zeppelin’s Jimmy Page and John Paul Jones, when Grohl went behind the drum kit at Hawkins took the mic).

As an influential band member, he co-wrote many of the band’s records.

Taylor Hawkins cause of death

Foo Fighters announced his demise via a Twitter post but didn’t detail the cause of his death.

The band said in its Twitter statement that they were devastated by Taylor Hawkins’ ‘tragic and untimely’ death.

“The Foo Fighters family is devastated by the tragic and untimely loss of our beloved Taylor Hawkins. His musical spirit and infectious laughter will live on with all of us forever,” the statement said.

“Our hearts go out to his wife, children and family, and we ask that their privacy be treated with the utmost respect in this unimaginably difficult time,” it added.

The band was scheduled to perform in Bogota, Colombia on Friday night.

Was Taylor Hawkins married? Did Taylor Hawkins have children?

Taylor Hawkins was married to Alison Hawkins from 2005 until his death. The couple had three children; Oliver Shane Hawkins, Everleigh Hawkins and Annabelle Hawkins.

Taylor Hawkins net worth

Taylor Hawkins had an estimated net worth of $20 million at the time of his death.

When is Taylor Hawkins funeral?

The band didn’t announce Taylor Hawkins’ funeral and burial service while announcing his death on Twitter. The burial ceremony for the drummer will soon be announced.
