Chloe Stott Utah Death Founder and Owner of Clover Skin Bar Died in a Crash

Chloe and Parker Stott were heading Home for the Holidays when a tragic accident claimed Chloe’s life and left Parker Critical. GoFundMe page

Chloe Cook Stott, the founder and owner of Clover Skin Bar, died in a tragic crash on her way home for Christmas Holidays on Wednesday, December 20, 2023. She was traveling with her husband, Parker Stott, from Utah to Arizona when the accident happened. Parker Stott received severe injuries and is under intense care, fighting for his life.

What happened to Chloe and Parker Stott?

Chloe and Parker Stott were heading home to Arizona from Utah on Wednesday, December 20, 2023, to spend Christmas with their families. The couple had just found out that they were pregnant with their first-ever baby and were excited to share the news with their loved one over Christmas.

But fate took a twisted turn, and they met with a tragic accident at 8:30 p.m. on Wednesday. The tragic accident claimed Chloe’s life along with their unborn baby boy. Parker was rushed to the Arizona Burn Center, where he is currently under intense care, fighting for his life.

A GoFundMe page has been established to help the family with medical bills, financial support, and funeral services, among many other reasons. The target is set at 75,000 dollars. Over 43,000 dollars have been raised within three hours. It is requested that all donate generously to the fundraiser to help the family during this crucial time.

Who was Chloe Stott?

Chloe Cook Stott was the founder and owner of Clover Skin Bar, a medical spa in Northern Utah. She was an acne and aging specialist who started her business at the age of 20 after graduating from aesthetics school in August 2020.

As her business grew, Chloe, along with her husband, inaugurated a large medical spa, Clover Skin Bar, and hired a team of Master Estheticians who specialized in acne and age prevention in 2022. The Clover Skin Bar announced the sad news of her passing in a story on their Instagram page, saying that the medical spa will be closed for the week following an unexpected tragedy.

“Due to an unexpected tragedy, Clover Skin Bar will be closed for the remainder of the week. Please send thoughts and prayers.”

The online community has outpoured support for the family, posting tributes on social media and offering overwhelming support for the family during this incredibly challenging time. We offer our heartfelt condolences to the family and friends and our continued prayers for Parker’s speedy recovery. May she rest in peace.
