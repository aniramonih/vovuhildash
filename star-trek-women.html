The Hottest Women of Star Trek Over the Years

The most famous and beautiful women of Star Trek have captivated fans for decades. From the original 1966 show to modern-day incarnations, these female characters are as diverse and enjoyable as their male counterparts. While each woman has her own unique appeal, they all share a common desire: to explore the universe beyond our own.

Kirk and Picard, the two most iconic captains of the series, have had their fair share of female sidekicks. From Spock’s Vulcan-human relationship with Lt. Nyota Uhura to Deanna Troi’s strong woman-of-action instincts on board the Enterprise, these characters bring a level of depth and complexity that can only be found in strong female characters.

Moreover, the most beautiful women of Star Trek have been able to capture fans with their alluring looks and outfits. Who were the most attractive and fascinating women of StarTrek over the past several decades? Let’s find out!

Seven of Nine

Seven of Nine is undoubtedly one of the hottest female actors to ever grace the Star Trek universe. Played by Jeri Ryan, Seven of Nine was an Astrometrics Officer on board the USS Voyager. Not only was she gorgeous on the outside, but her wit and intelligence were also unmatched. She quickly became a fan favorite character and remains unforgettable even today!

T’Pol

Enterprise, played by Jolene Blalock, was an incredibly hot Science Officer who could often be seen in form-fitting uniforms. Along with being sensual, T’Pol was also known for her strength and logical reasoning – making her easily one of the hottest characters in the series.

Carol Marcus

Alice Eve’s portrayal of Carol Marcus in 2013’s Star Trek Into Darkness was an instant hit. As a Science Officer, she not only had brains but brawn as well. Her no-nonsense attitude and beauty made her a key asset to the team and a fan favorite.

Jadzia Dax

Terry Farrell brought Jadzia Dax to life on Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. A scientist by trade, Dax’s intelligence and beauty made her one of the most memorable characters in the series. She was strong yet compassionate, making her even more attractive to viewers.

Kamala

Famke Janssen wowed fans with her portrayal of Kamala on The Next Generation. With her exotic looks and enigmatic personality, she quickly became one of the most popular female characters in the series. Her intelligence and charm had fans eager to find out what would happen next with Kamala.

Tonia Barrows

Emily Banks made quite an impression as Tonia Barrows on The Next Generation. As a Starfleet Officer, she was always at the forefront of any mission and her strength and determination made her an attractive character for many viewers. Not only that, but Tonia’s beauty also won over Trek fans worldwide!

Droxine

The sultry and mysterious Droxine, played by Diana Ewing, was a memorable character on the original series. She had an alluring presence that kept viewers guessing and her boldness made her stand out. Although she only appeared in one episode, she left an unforgettable impression on fans!

Amanda Cole

Noa Tishby brought Amanda Cole to life in the Enterprise episode Harbinger. As a Starfleet Officer, Cole was an important part of the mission but it was her beauty and intelligence that made her stand out from the crowd. Her strength and courage endeared her to many Trekkies who could relate to her character’s journey.

Nyota Uhura

Often referred to as one of the most iconic characters in the Star Trek universe, Nyota Uhura was played by Zoe Saldana in the 2009 movie reboot. As a Communications Officer, Uhura had an important role to play but she also became known for her strong personality and beauty. She was a leader among the crew and her courage inspired many.

Ishara Yar

Beth Toussaint’s portrayal of Ishara Yar on The Next Generation added mystery and intrigue to the show. With her exotic looks and bold attitude, she quickly became a fan favorite character. Not only was she beautiful, but Ishara also had strength and bravery in spades – making her one of the hottest female characters in Star Trek history!

Robin Lefler

In perhaps one of its most memorable episodes, Ashley Judd played Robin Lefler on The Next Generation. As a Mission Specialist, Lefler’s intelligence often saved the day but it was her beauty that made fans take notice. Her compassion towards her fellow crewmates was endearing and Lefler quickly became one of the series’ most beloved characters.

Deanna Troi

Marina Sirtis’ portrayal of Deanna Troi in The Next Generation was both powerful and beautiful. As the ship’s Counselor, she often provided much-needed advice and comfort to her crewmates – making her a key part of the mission as well as an alluring character for viewers. Her strength and intelligence made her stand out from other female characters in the series.

Vash

Jennifer Hetrick played Vash on The Next Generation, a charming archaeologist with a knack for getting into trouble! Not only did she have beauty, but Vash also had brains – something that made fans love her even more. Her courage and wit were legendary and

her character was truly one of the most attractive women in Star Trek.

Leeta

Chase Masterson brought Leeta to life on Deep Space Nine as a Dabo girl who eventually married Doctor Bashir, making her a memorable part of the crew. She had an endearing presence and winning smile that made viewers fall in love with her instantly! Despite being quite young, Leeta proved herself to be strong and capable – making her an incredibly attractive female character in Star Trek.

Carolyn Palamas

Leslie Parrish played Carolyn Palamas on The Original Series episode Who Mourns for Adonais? Her beauty and charm stole the hearts of many viewers, and her intelligence made her a powerful female character. Not only was she an actress, model and activist, but Carolyn Palamas also had the strength and courage to go on a mission with Captain Kirk – making her one of the most attractive characters in Star Trek!

Kelinda

Barbara Bouchet portrayed Kelinda on The Original Series episode By Any Other Name. She had an alluring presence that held viewers’ attention and her boldness made her stand out from other characters in the series. Although she only appeared in one episode, Kelinda left an unforgettable impression on fans who admired her beauty and intelligence!

Hoshi Sato

Linda Park played Hoshi Sato on Enterprise, a linguist who joined the crew and quickly became a fan favorite. She was an intelligent and beautiful character with a charming personality that shined through in every episode. Despite being relatively new to the Star Trek universe, Hoshi quickly proved her worth – making her one of the most attractive female characters.

Bronwyn Gail Robinson

Teri Hatcher’s portrayal of Bronwyn Gail Robinson on The Next Generation made viewers take notice! As Transporter Chief, she had great strength and intelligence but it was her beauty that truly captivated fans. Her courage and determination made her stand out from other characters in the series, and her charm made her one of the hottest female stars in Star Trek.

Remember the Lovely Ladies of Strek Trek

No matter what era they appeared in or what position they held, these women are all incredibly beautiful and talented actresses. From Uhura to Vina, their presence in Star Trek has earned them a special place in the hearts of many fans – making them some of the hottest female stars in Star Trek history!
