Forget New Yorks 2017 Fashion Week. The 14th NY Pet Fashion Show is Much More Adorable and Amazin

New York’s 2017 Fashion Week would not be complete without a literal cat walk. The 14th Annual NY Pet Fashion Show presented by TropiClean at the Hotel Pennsylvania commenced Thursday February 9th with over 150 high fashion four legged models. Taking place ahead of the Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show, the show boasts an all-inclusive cast of animal models, made all the more apparent with the Kennels cat-controversy happening this year.

“Global Couture for Animal Rescue” was the evening’s theme. Pets dressed in elaborate costumes inspired by cultures around the globe strutted down the catwalk. The show is billed as the country’s largest charitable pet fashion show. Tickets for the event ranged from 60$ for a single ticket at the door to table reservations upwards of 750$ with proceeds from the show donated to the Mayor’s Alliance for NYC’s Animals. Good news for the charity since more than 700 people showed up for the event.

Check out some cute cat and dog pics in elaborate outfits below.

Pet fashion designer Anthony Rubio walked with 9 year old model and actress Briella Simpson and his two Chihuahuas Bogie and Kimba. They were representing the United Kingdom with all four wearing Rubio’s McQueen inspired designs.

Rubio also showcased his 2017 line on the Art Hearts Fashion platform at the Angel Orensanz Foundation on February 13th.

Yeyush and Renzo the Chihuahua’s are pictured above representing France. Darlene Hachey designed the outfits while the sunglasses are by pet fashion designer and animal rights activist Ada Nieves.

Princess Pussywillow makes an appearance as a literal Cat in a Hat. Princess Pussywillow’s entourage included feline model companion Princess Snowflake and three generations of women representing Armenia. The clothing, all handmade by the women, were embroidered with the date April 24, 1915 to commemorate the Armenian genocide of 1915. The statement alongside the women’s beautiful handiwork reminds us of the poignant moments which exist within fashion week.

Pug models Oliver and Suri represent China with their mom, Sierra Schast.

Fitness guru Summer Strand and Chihuahua April Moon dress up like Tsarina’s to represent Russia.

Pet fashion designer and celebrity pet groomer Miguel Rodriguez and furbaby Venus wear high fashion sugar skull inspired headdresses.

Puerto Rico represented by this bright yellow duo.

Merie the Chihuahua representing Costa Rica with her bright blue couture.

Irish honor by Debbie Nap and Queen Guinevere the artistically bred Yorkie.

Gia, Pia, Tia and Mia make an appearance. Rollin’ up in their bright pink Mercedez-Bonez the Yorkie quadruplets watch the pet festivities take place.
