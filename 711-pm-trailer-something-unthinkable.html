7:11 PM Trailer: Something Unthinkable!

Article by Satya B Published on: 4:23 pm, 29 June 2023

The first look as well as the first single from the sci-fi thriller 7:11 PM received a good response. Saahas and Deepika played the lead roles in the movie helmed by Chaitu Madala. The teaser of the movie was launched a few days ago to a positive response. The film’s theatrical trailer has been unveiled today.

How do you feel when you sleep in one place and wake up at another place which is far away from your locality? That’s the situation of the protagonist who started his journey the other day in his hometown of Hamsaladeevi and when he wakes up he is in Melbourne in Australia. There are different layers to this story. An alien aircraft that was landed on Earth is on a mission and a politician is also on the same or different mission.

The story, the taking, and the technical standards are something unthinkable. It cannot be called a small-time film, just because it doesn’t have a star cast and popular technicians associated with it. The trailer surely ignites our excitement to watch the movie.

7:11 PM will release through Mythri on July 7th.
