Net Worth Of Joss Stone In 2021

Joss Stone has an estimated net worth of $8 million with a stunning acting and singing career.

As per CelebrityNetWorth, in 2012, Joss Stone had a net worth of $10 million, making her the fifth richest British musician under 30. But, she spent her money on buying herself out of her contract with record company EMI. She spent nearly 100% of her net worth on purchasing her career freedom.

Joss Stone rose to fame after her debut album 'The Soul Sessions' in 2003, which made the 2004 Mercury Prize shortlist, and she was 16-years-old at that time. 

After gaining massive attention from the public, she also became the youngest British female singer to top the UK Album Chart. Her second album, Mind Body & Soul (2004), topped the UK Albums Chart for one week. 

Joss's third album, Introducing Joss Stone (2007), achieved gold record status by the RIAA and yielded the second-highest debut for a British female solo artist on the Billboard 200. 

She has released her fourth and fifth album Colour Me Free! (2009), and LP1 (2011), which reached the top 10 on Billboard. Through her fabulous career as an artist, she had sold 17 million albums worldwide and became one of the best-selling soul artists of the 2000s. 

Joss has won two BRIT Awards and one Grammy Award out of five nominations. 

Joss Stone has pursued her hand at acting as well. She made her film acting debut in 2006 on the film Eragon as she characterised the role as Angela.

In 2014, she was part of the TV series El Hormiguero MX. The actress Joss has also appeared in short videos such as Joss Stone: Fell in love with a Boy (2004), Joss Stone: Super Duper Love (2004), Joss Stone: You Had Me (2004), Joss Stone: Right to Be Wrong (2005), Joss Stone: Baby Baby Baby (2009), Joss Stone: Stuck on You (2015), and You Okay Doc? (2020).

She is also active in charity work through The Joss Stone foundation, founded in 2000. The foundation worked with the global network of charities, supported the people, and delivered locally and nationally in 200 countries. 

Who Is Joss Stone? Age, Family, & Height

Her father, Richard Stoker, owns a fruit and nut import-export business. Her mother, Wendy Skillin Stoker, worked as Joss Stone's manager until October 2004. She has three siblings named Harry Stoker (Older Brother), Lucy Stoker (Older Sister), and Daniel Stoker (Younger Brother).
